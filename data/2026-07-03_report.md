## 📄 论文 1 - 相关性: 55%
**标题:** Learning When to Listen: Gated Affect Fusion for Human Motion Prediction
**作者:** Jingni Huang
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.00296

**相关性说明:** The paper is relevant to computer graphics in that it deals with human motion analysis, but it focuses on motion prediction (forecasting future poses from past observations) rather than video-to-3D motion generation. The methods (MediaPipe, facial emotion extraction) involve extracting 2D visual features from video, but it's not addressing the core pain point of converting 2D video representations to 3D motion space. The research profile specifically wants methods like video generation models (Wanxiang, Seedance) and two-stage video-to-motion pipelines, whereas this paper uses a completely different approach (Transformer-based prediction with gating mechanisms). While both deal with human motion, the application domain and methodology are substantially different from what's needed.

**摘要:** 本文研究了在自然场景视频中，面部情感特征对人体运动预测的实用性和时间局限性。提出Gated Affect Transformer (GAT)来动态调节跨模态信息流，发现 naive 早期跨模态融合会降低预测精度，而门控机制能自适应控制情感流，在短中期窗口(30帧)内提供有限的预测线索。面部情感应被视为补充性行为线索而非主导因素。


## 📄 论文 2 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** Progressive Pose-Guided 4D Animal Reconstruction from Monocular Video
**作者:** Siyuan Li, Weiying Chen, Yilin Wang
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.00157

**相关性说明:** This paper addresses 4D animal reconstruction from monocular video using 3D Gaussian Splatting, which is a DIFFERENT application domain from the research profile's focus on video-to-3D motion generation (specifically human motion). While both deal with video-to-3D conversion, the end goal differs: this paper focuses on geometry reconstruction of animal models, not motion generation. The pain points partially overlap (extracting 3D from 2D video), but the methods (3D Gaussian Splatting vs. diffusion models like Wanxiang/Seedance) and application domain (animal reconstruction vs. human motion generation) are substantially different. The paper is relevant to computer graphics but not to the specific research direction of video-to-motion generation.

**摘要:** 提出了一种基于3D Gaussian Splatting的渐进式测试时优化框架，用于从单目视频重建高保真4D动物模型，通过对称感知时序编码和部件条件变形机制解耦关节姿态与非刚性变形。


## 📄 论文 3 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** LIST3R: Long-sequence Instance-aware 3D Reconstruction
**作者:** Jing Gao, Wei Wang, Feiran Wang
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.00375

**相关性说明:** This paper addresses 3D SCENE RECONSTRUCTION from long video sequences, which is a different application domain from the research profile's focus on 3D MOTION GENERATION. While both deal with video-to-3D conversion, the goal is fundamentally different: reconstruction creates static 3D scenes/objects, while the profile seeks to generate dynamic 3D motion sequences (like human motion). The methods also don't match - the profile uses video generation models (Wanxiang/Seedance) and two-stage video-to-motion pipelines, whereas this paper uses instance tracking and geometric reconstruction. The paper is relevant to computer graphics but not to the specific motion generation research area within it.

**摘要:** LIST3R是一种基于实例感知的长时间序列3D重建框架，灵感来源于人类围绕稳定可识别物体组织空间记忆的方式。该方法通过实例锚点重新连接碎片化的子序列，将局部观测整合为连贯的全局3D场景。

**相关性说明:** This paper is about general keypoint detection in images using visual/text prompts, which is a computer vision task. While it uses some related visual feature extraction, it does NOT address the specific research profile needs: (1) No video processing - it's for static images; (2) No 3D motion generation; (3) No video-to-motion conversion pipeline; (4) Not specifically for computer graphics animation/motion applications. **摘要:** 本文提出了MegaKPT大规模统一关键点数据集和GKDT通用关键点检测模型，支持视觉提示、文本提示或两者结合，在22个测试集上展现强泛化能力，大多数类别PCK@0.1准确率超过90%。
