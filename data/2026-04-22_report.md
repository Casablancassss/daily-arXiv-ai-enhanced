## 📄 论文 1 - 相关性: 78%
**标题:** EgoMotion: Hierarchical Reasoning and Diffusion for Egocentric Vision-Language Motion Generation
**作者:** Ruibing Hou, Mingyue Zhou, Yuwei Gui
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.19105

**相关性说明:** The paper directly addresses the research profile's core concerns: it uses VLM for visual reasoning and diffusion models for 3D motion generation from egocentric (first-person) visual inputs. The two-stage design explicitly tackles "bridging 2D video priors to 3D motion synthesis" through hierarchical feature extraction (VLM projecting to motion primitives), and temporal consistency is achieved via iterative diffusion denoising. While the egocentric context (likely robot/agent motion) differs slightly from human character animation, both fall under 3D motion generation in computer graphics, making the methods directly transferable.

**摘要:** 本文提出EgoMotion框架，通过认知推理与运动生成的两阶段分离设计，解决第一人称视角下视觉-语言引导的运动生成中语义推理与运动学建模之间的梯度冲突问题。


## 📄 论文 2 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** Face Anything: 4D Face Reconstruction from Any Image Sequence
**作者:** Umut Kocasari, Simon Giebenhain, Richard Shaw
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.19702

**相关性说明:** This paper focuses on 4D facial reconstruction from image sequences, which is a specific application in face modeling rather than general 3D motion generation. While it addresses temporal consistency (one of the pain points), the application domain differs significantly - it's about reconstructing/tracking existing facial geometry rather than generating new motions from 2D video priors. The methods (canonical facial point prediction, facial depth estimation) are specialized for faces rather than the general motion synthesis methods mentioned in the profile (Video Generation Models, VLMs, 3D Motion Generation Models). The paper is more relevant to computer vision/facial animation rather than the computer graphics motion generation pipeline described.

**摘要:** 提出一种基于规范面部点预测的高保真4D人脸重建统一方法。该方法将密集跟踪和动态重建转化为规范重建问题，使用transformer模型联合预测深度和规范面部坐标，实现了时间一致的几何和可靠的对应。

## 📄 论文 4 - 相关性: 30%
**标题:** How Far Are Video Models from True Multimodal Reasoning?
**作者:** Xiaotian Zhang, Jianhui Wei, Yuan Wang
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.19193

**相关性说明:** This paper is about evaluating video models' reasoning capabilities using benchmarks, not about 3D motion generation from 2D video. While it mentions Seedance and touches on video understanding, the core application domain is different - it's about AI/ML evaluation rather than computer graphics motion synthesis. The pain points in the profile (extracting features for 3D motion, bridging 2D video to 3D motion, temporal consistency) are not addressed. Only surface-level method overlap (Seedance is mentioned) exists.

**摘要:** 本文提出CLVG-Bench评估框架，通过视频生成中的上下文学习来探测视频模型的零样本推理能力。该框架包含1000多个高质量人工标注样本，涵盖6个类别和47个子类别，并提出自适应视频评估器(AVE)与人类专家感知对齐。


## 📄 论文 5 - 相关性: 30%
**标题:** CreatiParser: Generative Image Parsing of Raster Graphic Designs into Editable Layers
**作者:** Weidong Chen, Dexiang Hong, Zhendong Mao
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.19632

**相关性说明:** While both papers use VLM and diffusion models, the application domains are fundamentally different. The research profile focuses on 3D MOTION GENERATION from 2D video inputs (temporal motion synthesis), whereas CreatiParser addresses static IMAGE PARSING for graphic design decomposition (converting raster designs into editable layers). The core pain points in the profile involve temporal consistency and bridging 2D video-to-3D motion, but this paper focuses on layer separation for design editing. The methods (VLM, diffusion) are applied to a completely different task - static design parsing rather than motion generation from visual inputs.

**摘要:** 提出一种将图形设计图像分解为可编辑文本、背景和贴图层级的混合生成框架，使用视觉语言模型解析文本，多分支扩散架构生成背景和贴纸，并引入ParserReward和GRPO进行人类偏好对齐，在两个数据集上实现23.7%的性能提升。
