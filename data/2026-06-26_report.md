## 📄 论文 1 - 相关性: 50%
**标题:** Follow Your Track: Precise Skeleton Animation Controlled by 3D Trajectories
**作者:** Yueting Liu, Yanqin Jiang, Nian Liu
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.25344

**相关性说明:** The paper addresses skeleton animation via 3D trajectories, which is moderately relevant to the Text-to-Motion research area. It shares the same general domain (Computer Graphics - 3D motion generation) and addresses temporal consistency (one related pain point). However, it uses 3D trajectory control as input rather than text or video, which is a different modality than the research profile's focus on text-to-motion or video-to-motion. The method (ACT framework) is not the same as text-to-motion generation models, and it doesn't address text-video consistency or VLM-based quality filtering.

**摘要:** ACT是一种基于轨迹条件的拓扑通用骨骼动画框架，使用骨骼作为紧凑表示结合3D点轨迹作为显式运动引导，解决4D生成中计算成本高、时间一致性差、控制能力有限的问题。


## 📄 论文 2 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** Causal-rCM: A Unified Teacher-Forcing and Self-Forcing Open Recipe for Autoregressive Diffusion Distillation in Streaming Video Generation and Interactive World Models
**作者:** Kaiwen Zheng, Guande He, Min Zhao
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.25473

**相关性说明:** This paper focuses on TEXT-TO-VIDEO generation (T2V), not TEXT-TO-MOTION generation. While both use diffusion models, they are fundamentally different application domains: video generation produces 2D video frames, while motion generation produces 3D skeletal animations for humans/robots. Although the paper mentions Wanxiang (also called Wan2.1), it uses it for streaming video generation rather than motion generation. The methods and benchmarks (VBench-T2V) are entirely focused on video quality, not motion quality or motion-text consistency. This is a case of using similar methods (diffusion) but in the wrong application domain.

**摘要:** 将rCM扩散蒸馏框架扩展到自回归视频扩散领域，提出了Causal-rCM统一算法。通过教师强制一致性模型(CM)与自回归分布匹配蒸馏(DMD)的结合，配合定制的FlashAttention-2 JVP内核，首次实现了连续时间CM在自回归视频扩散中的应用，在仅使用合成数据训练的情况下实现了最先进的流式视频生成性能。


## 📄 论文 3 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** MVTrack4Gen: Multi-View Point Tracking as Geometric Supervision for 4D Video Generation
**作者:** JoungBin Lee, Jaewoo Jung, Jongmin Lee
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.26087

**相关性说明:** This paper addresses 4D video generation (novel view video synthesis), which is a DIFFERENT application domain from Text-to-Motion Generation (text-to-3D human motion). While both involve diffusion models and motion, the goal is fundamentally different: MVTrack4Gen focuses on geometric consistency in multi-view video synthesis, not on text-to-motion generation or 2D video-to-3D motion conversion. The paper doesn't address text-to-motion consistency, VLM-based quality filtering, or the specific pain points mentioned in the research profile. It uses multi-view point tracking as supervision, which is unrelated to the motion generation methods needed here.

**摘要:** 本文提出MVTrack4Gen框架，通过引入多视角点跟踪作为额外的几何和运动监督信号，增强相机条件化新视角视频扩散模型的几何一致性和运动保真度。


## 📄 论文 4 - 相关性: 30%
**标题:** Physics Question Scene Graph: Fine-grained Evaluation of Physical Plausibility in Text-to-Video Generation
**作者:** Atin Pothiraj, Jaemin Cho, Yue Zhang
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.25306

**相关性说明:** The paper addresses Text-to-Video generation evaluation, which is a fundamentally different application domain from Text-to-Motion Generation. While both use VLM and diffusion models, the target output is completely different: 2D video vs 3D human motion. The pain points are also different - this paper focuses on physical plausibility in videos, not quality control of video-to-motion conversion or motion-text consistency. Although it mentions Wanxiang (also in the research profile), the evaluation framework targets video generation quality, not motion generation quality. A paper about "diffusion for image generation" getting low score for "diffusion for human motion generation" applies here - the application domains are distinct.

**摘要:** 本文提出了Physics Question Scene Graph (PQSG)，一种基于层次化问题的新型评估管道，用于评估视频生成模型对物理定律的遵循程度。通过FinePhyEval数据集验证，表明PQSG与人类判断的相关性高于现有方法。


## 📄 论文 5 - 相关性: 25%
**标题:** KidRisk: Benchmark Dataset for Children Dangerous Action Recognition
**作者:** Minh-Kha Nguyen, Trung-Hieu Do, Kim Anh Phung
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.25298

**相关性说明:** The paper is about action recognition (classifying dangerous actions from videos/images), which is a different application domain from text-to-motion generation (generating 3D human motion from text/video). Both use Vision-Language Models superficially, but the core task is recognition vs generation - these are opposite directions in the motion understanding pipeline. The pain points in the research profile (video generation instability, quality control of motion conversion, consistency between generated motion and text) are not addressed by a paper focused on recognizing pre-existing actions in children.

**摘要:** 本文构建了一个名为KidRisk的新型数据集，包含2500个儿童行为短视频和10000张危险行为图像，并基于视觉语言模型开发了儿童动作分类和危险行为识别方法。
