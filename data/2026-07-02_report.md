## 📄 论文 1 - 相关性: 65%
**标题:** One Video, One World: Turning Monocular Video into Physical 4D Scenes
**作者:** Junhao Chen, Boran Zhang, Mingjin Chen
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.31388

**相关性说明:** The paper is in Computer Graphics domain and addresses some pain points: it uses VLM for quality supervision, extracts 3D visual features from 2D video, and bridges 2D video to 3D representation. However, it's a RECONSTRUCTION method rather than a GENERATION method - it reconstructs 4D mesh scenes from video rather than synthesizing new 3D motion. The paper focuses on scene-level physical simulation rather than motion synthesis for characters/robots. It's moderately relevant but differs in core objective (reconstruction vs generation) and output type (scene meshes vs motion data).

**摘要:** OVOW是首个无需训练的、从单目视频重建实例级仿真就绪4D网格场景的系统。该系统解决了现有4D重建方法输出（隐式场、高斯原语、点云）缺乏水密拓扑、实例分离和标准化物理接口的问题，可直接用于物理仿真和具身AI。

**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.31981

**相关性说明:** While this paper addresses 3D human animation in Computer Graphics and tackles 2D-to-3D mapping, it doesn't align well with the specific research profile. The profile focuses on extracting guidance from VIDEO priors and using Vision-Language Models for quality supervision, but LUNA uses 2D control signals (images, keypoints, sketches) without video-specific processing or VLM integration. LUNA is about neural animation beyond LBS skinning, not about video-to-3D motion synthesis. The paper is moderately relevant as it's in the same general domain (3D human motion/animation) but doesn't address the specific pain points of video-based motion extraction and VLM-based quality control mentioned in the research profile.

**摘要:** LUNA是一个无需线性混合蒙皮(LBS)的通用神经动画模型,直接从多种2D控制信号(图像、关键点、草图等)映射到3D高斯变形,实现逼真的人类动作动画。

This paper uses Gaussian Splatting for rendering rather than the specified methods (VLM, Video Generation Models). Moderate relevance as it deals with 3D human representation but uses different methods and addresses a different input modality.

**摘要:** PointSplat是一种从输入视图实时生成紧凑高质量3D人体表示的新方法，通过直接从点集推理高斯原语，避免了传统方法中的视图间冗余。


## 📄 论文 4 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** JacobianAvatar: Temporally Consistent Semi-rigid Avatar Reconstruction from a Monocular Video
**作者:** Changyeon Won, Min-Gyu Park, Seonghwan Park
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.31115

**相关性说明:** This paper addresses human avatar reconstruction from monocular video, which is related to Computer Graphics but differs from the research profile's focus on 3D MOTION SYNTHESIS. The profile seeks methods for generating new 3D motions from video (like Wanxiang, Seedance), while this paper reconstructs existing human geometry/appearance. The methods are also different: the profile uses VLMs and video generation models, while this paper uses neural Jacobian fields and Poisson equations for deformation modeling. This is a case of using similar methods in a related but distinct application domain - avatar reconstruction vs. motion generation.

**摘要:** 提出利用神经雅可比场(NJFs)表示半刚性变形，通过求解泊松方程预测姿态相关的变形，解决单目视角下生成逼真人类avatar的挑战。


## 📄 论文 5 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** Learning to Deny: Action Denial in Multimodal Large Language Models
**作者:** Raiyaan Abdullah, Shehreen Azad, Yogesh Singh Rawat
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.31187

**相关性说明:** This paper focuses on action denial capabilities in Multimodal Large Language Models for video understanding, which is in the domain of video analysis/action recognition rather than Computer Graphics or 3D motion synthesis. While it uses Vision-Language Models (one of the methods listed), the application domain is completely different - it's about causal reasoning to determine if actions occurred in videos, not about extracting 3D visual features from video for motion generation, quality supervision for graphics, or bridging 2D video to 3D motion representation. The paper addresses video understanding rather than any 3D graphics or motion synthesis tasks specified in the research profile.

**摘要:** UCF101-AD是一个用于评估多模态大语言模型（MLLMs）动作否定能力的大规模基准数据集。研究发现当前最先进的MLLMs在动作识别上准确率超过85%，但在动作否定任务上准确率低于50%，揭示了模型无法进行因果推理判断动作是否真正发生的关键缺陷。提出CausalAct方法通过因果图形式关联上下文、交互和运动，显著减少误报。
