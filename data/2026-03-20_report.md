It's in the same general field (Computer Graphics/Video) but addresses a different problem than the stated research needs.

**摘要:** SGC是一种评估动态生成视频中3D空间几何一致性的新指标，通过测量从不同局部区域估计的多个相机姿态之间的分歧来量化几何一致性。


## 📄 论文 2 - 相关性: 45%
**标题:** Bridging Semantic and Kinematic Conditions with Diffusion-based Discrete Motion Tokenizer
**作者:** Chenyang Gu, Mingyuan Zhang, Haozhe Xie
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.19227

**相关性说明:** While this paper addresses 3D human motion generation in Computer Graphics, it focuses on text-to-motion generation with kinematic constraints, NOT on extracting 3D motion from 2D video priors. The research profile specifically emphasizes extracting 3D motion from 2D video and using visual guidance features to drive motion synthesis - which this paper does not address. The paper combines semantic and kinematic conditions for motion generation, but lacks any 2D visual/video component, quality control of video generation outputs, or VLM integration that the profile requires.

**摘要:** 本文提出一个三阶段框架，结合离散token生成和连续扩散模型的优点，用于人体动作生成。该框架包含感知、规划和控制三个阶段，核心创新是MoTok——一种基于扩散的离散动作tokenizer。


## 📄 论文 3 - 相关性: 45%
**标题:** Generation Models Know Space: Unleashing Implicit 3D Priors for Scene Understanding
**作者:** Xianjin Wu, Dingkang Liang, Tianrui Feng
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.19235

**相关性说明:** This paper addresses spatial reasoning and 3D scene understanding for MLLMs using video diffusion models, which is a different application domain from the research profile's focus on 3D motion generation from 2D video. While both deal with 2D→3D and use video generation models/VLMs, the output goals differ: scene understanding/geometry reasoning vs. 3D motion synthesis. The paper touches on extracting implicit 3D priors from video models, which is tangentially related to the "bridging 2D-3D gap" pain point, but the primary application (scene understanding for robotics/embodied AI) is distinct from computer graphics motion generation.

**摘要:** VEGA-3D通过将预训练视频扩散模型重构为潜在世界模拟器，为多模态大语言模型注入空间推理能力。该方法从中间噪声层级提取时空特征，通过token级自适应门控融合机制整合语义表示，无需显式3D监督即可增强MLLMs的几何推理能力。


## 📄 论文 4 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** Inst4DGS: Instance-Decomposed 4D Gaussian Splatting with Multi-Video Label Permutation Learning
**作者:** Yonghan Lee, Dinesh Manocha
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.18402

**相关性说明:** This paper is about 4D Gaussian Splatting for dynamic scene reconstruction and instance segmentation, which falls within Computer Graphics but addresses a different application domain than the research profile. While both deal with 3D/4D content, this paper focuses on multi-view video-based 4D scene rendering and instance tracking (improving PSNR and mIoU metrics), not on video generation models, 3D motion synthesis from 2D priors, or quality control of generated video outputs. The methods (4DGS, Sinkhorn for label permutation) are unrelated to diffusion-based video generation or motion generation models. Only tangential connection exists in processing video as input, but the goal is reconstruction rather than generation or motion synthesis.

**摘要:** Inst4DGS是一种实例分解的4D高斯溅射方法，采用长-horizon每高斯轨迹来处理动态场景中的实例跟踪和分解问题。


## 📄 论文 5 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** FILT3R: Latent State Adaptive Kalman Filter for Streaming 3D Reconstruction
**作者:** Seonghyun Jin, Jong Chul Ye
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.18493

**相关性说明:** This paper is about 3D RECONSTRUCTION (estimating 3D geometry from video), not 3D MOTION GENERATION (synthesizing new 3D motion from video priors). These are fundamentally different application domains in computer graphics. The research profile focuses on video-to-motion synthesis, quality control of video generation outputs, and using visual features to drive 3D motion synthesis - while FILT3R addresses long-term stability in streaming 3D reconstruction via Kalman filtering. Although both deal with video-to-3D, reconstruction (recovery) is different from generation (synthesis). The methods also don't match - profile uses video generation models and VLMs, while this paper uses Kalman filters for state estimation.

**摘要:** FILT3R通过将3D重建的潜在状态更新建模为token空间的卡尔曼滤波，在线估计过程噪声以自适应调整记忆保留与新观测的平衡，无需训练即可提升长时域3D重建稳定性。
