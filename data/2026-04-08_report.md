## 📄 论文 1 - 相关性: 30%
**标题:** HumANDiff: Articulated Noise Diffusion for Motion-Consistent Human Video Generation
**作者:** Tao Hu, Varun Jampani
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.05961

**相关性说明:** The paper is about 2D human VIDEO generation (creating videos), while the research profile is about 3D Human MOTION generation (creating 3D motion data/skeletons). Although both involve human motion, the application domains are fundamentally different. The profile focuses on 2D-to-3D motion reconstruction and 3D pose recovery from 2D visual cues, but this paper focuses on generating 2D videos from motion inputs using diffusion. The use of 3D body templates is for noise sampling in the video generation pipeline, not for motion reconstruction. This is a case of using similar methods (diffusion) but in a different application domain.

**摘要:** HumANDiff是一个增强人类运动控制的人体视频生成框架，通过关节噪声采样、联合外观-运动学习和几何运动一致性损失三个创新设计，无需修改扩散模型架构即可实现高质量可控人体视频生成。


## 📄 论文 2 - 相关性: 30%

**作者:** Yukun Wang, Ruihuang Li, Jiale Tao
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.06010

**相关性说明:** While this paper uses diffusion-based video generation (mentioned in the research profile), its application domain is completely different. OmniCamera focuses on general video generation with camera motion control, not 3D human motion generation, 2D-to-3D motion reconstruction, or human pose recovery. This is a case of "similar methods but wrong application domain" - the method (diffusion for video generation) is used for general video/camera control rather than human motion-specific applications.

**摘要:** OmniCamera 是一个统一的视频生成框架，通过解耦相机运动和场景动态内容实现灵活的视频生成控制。


## 📄 论文 3 - 相关性: 25%
**标题:** Purify-then-Align: Towards Robust Human Sensing under Modality Missing with Knowledge Distillation from Noisy Multimodal Teacher
**作者:** Pengcheng Weng, Yanyu Qian, Yangxin Xu
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.05584

**相关性说明:** This paper focuses on multimodal human sensing/pose estimation (computer vision) for handling missing modalities, NOT 3D Human Motion Generation (computer graphics). The application domain is fundamentally different - it's about perceiving human poses from real sensor data (RGB, depth, skeleton) rather than generating 3D motion. While it uses diffusion for knowledge distillation, this is for feature refinement in perception tasks, not for motion generation. The pain points addressed (modality missing, representation gap between heterogeneous data, noise/pollution) are different from the research profile's focus on 2D-to-3D motion conversion, temporal consistency, and quality control in generated videos.

**摘要:** 提出PTA框架，通过"先净化后对齐"策略解决多模态人体感知中缺失模态的挑战。


## 📄 论文 4 - 相关性: 25%
**标题:** FunRec: Reconstructing Functional 3D Scenes from Egocentric Interaction Videos
**作者:** Alexandros Delitzas, Chenyangguang Zhang, Alexey Gavryushin
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.05621

**相关性说明:** This paper is about 3D SCENE reconstruction from egocentric videos (reconstructing indoor scenes with articulated objects like doors, drawers), NOT 3D human motion generation. While both deal with 2D-to-3D reconstruction, the application domains are fundamentally different - scene/object reconstruction vs. human body motion generation. The paper focuses on discovering articulated parts and tracking object motion in environments, not human motion or pose recovery. This is a completely different research field within computer graphics.

**摘要:** FunRec是一种从第一人称RGB-D交互视频直接重建室内场景功能性3D数字孪生的方法，无需 CAD 先验或多状态捕捉，可自动发现铰接部件、估计运动参数、跟踪3D运动并重建仿真兼容网格。


## 📄 论文 5 - 相关性: 25%
**标题:** Towards Athlete Fatigue Assessment from Association Football Videos
**作者:** Xavier Bou, Nathan Correger, Alexandre Cloots
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.05636

**相关性说明:** This paper is about sports analytics (football/soccer player fatigue assessment) using trajectory extraction from broadcast videos. While it deals with motion analysis, it is NOT in the Computer Graphics / 3D Human Motion Generation domain. The application is sports performance analysis, not 3D human motion generation for graphics, animation, or virtual humans. Methods used (GSR trajectory reconstruction, kinematic analysis) are different from diffusion-based video generation, VLMs, or 3D motion generation models. The paper addresses athlete monitoring, not generating or assessing 3D human motion for graphics applications.

**摘要:** 研究探索将单目广播视频作为足球运动员疲劳监测的低成本工具，基于比赛状态重建方法提取球员轨迹，通过运动学处理算法获取速度加速度估计，构建加速-速度(A-S)轮廓作为疲劳相关性能指标，并在SoccerNet-GSR基准数据集上验证了该方法的可行性。
