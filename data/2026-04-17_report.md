## 📄 论文 1 - 相关性: 30%
**标题:** AnimationBench: Are Video Models Good at Character-Centric Animation?
**作者:** Leyi Wu, Pengjun Fang, Kai Sun
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.15299

**相关性说明:** This paper is about Image-to-Video (I2V) generation for animated videos, which is a completely different application domain from 3D Human Motion Generation. The research profile focuses on extracting and reconstructing 3D human motion from 2D videos, while AnimationBench focuses on generating 2D animated videos from images. Although both involve "motion" in some form, the goals and domains are entirely different: one deals with 3D motion reconstruction, the other deals with 2D animation synthesis. The paper uses VLM only for evaluation purposes, not for 3D motion quality supervision or feature extraction as required by the research profile. This represents a case of using similar methods (VLM) but in a wrong application domain.

**摘要:** 本文提出了AnimationBench，这是首个专门用于评估动画图像生成视频(I2V)的benchmark。针对现有视频基准过于侧重真实感、缺乏对动画特有风格(如夸张动作、角色一致性)评估的问题，该工作将动画十二原则和IP保护概念转化为可测量的评估维度。


## 📄 论文 2 - 相关性: 25%
**标题:** SynHAT: A Two-stage Coarse-to-Fine Diffusion Framework for Synthesizing Human Activity Traces
**作者:** Rongchao Xu, Lin Jiang, Dahai Yu
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.14705

**相关性说明:** 虽然该论文使用了扩散模型，但"Human Activity Traces"(HAT)指的是地理空间中的轨迹数据（GPS轨迹、出行模式），而非计算机图形学中的3D人体动作生成（骨骼动作、姿态序列）。两者应用领域完全不同：前者属于城市计算/隐私保护领域，后者属于计算机图形学/动画生成领域。论文解决的是轨迹合成中的时空依赖建模和计算效率问题，与3D人体动作从2D视频提取、跨模态对齐、视觉特征筛选等痛点无关。

**摘要:** SynHAT是一个基于时空去噪扩散模型的计算高效的两阶段HAT合成框架，通过粗粒度到细粒度的pipeline解决隐私保护下的人体活动轨迹合成问题，在多个城市数据上取得显著性能提升。


## 📄 论文 3 - 相关性: 25%
**标题:** One-shot Compositional 3D Head Avatars with Deformable Hair
**作者:** Yuan Sun, Xuan Wang, WeiLi Zhang
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.14782

**相关性说明:** 该论文专注于从单张图像构建3D头部虚拟数字人，重点是头发与面部的解耦和头发动态模拟（使用3DGS、PBD模拟等）。虽然属于计算机图形学领域，但应用场景是静态头部重建和渲染，而非三维人体动作生成。论文关注的是头发变形而非人体动作（姿态、动作序列）的生成和提取，与research profile中从2D视频提取3D人体动作、跨模态对齐等技术痛点完全不匹配。方法上使用的是3D高斯溅射、FLAME网格等渲染技术，而非视频生成模型或VLM特征筛选。因此属于不同的应用子领域，相关性很低。

**摘要:** 本文提出了一种从单张图像构建完整3D头部虚拟数字人的 compositional 方法，特别针对头发与面部的解耦以及头发动态效果的逼真模拟。


## 📄 论文 4 - 相关性: 25%
**标题:** Generative Data Augmentation for Skeleton Action Recognition
**作者:** Xu Dong, Wanqing Li, Anthony Adeyemi-Ejeye
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.14933

**相关性说明:** This paper is about skeleton action recognition (a computer vision classification task), while the research profile is about 3D human motion generation for computer graphics. These are fundamentally different application domains: recognition vs generation. The paper addresses data augmentation for improving action classification accuracy, not generating new 3D human motions from cross-modal inputs (text-video-3D). It doesn't use VLM for quality supervision, video generation models as 2D priors, or address cross-modal feature alignment - which are the core methods in the research profile.

**摘要:** 提出了一种基于条件生成的数据增强 pipeline，用于骨架动作识别。该方法使用 Transformer 编码器-解码器架构学习真实骨架序列的分布，可在有限样本情况下生成多样且高质量的骨架序列。


## 📄 论文 5 - 相关性: 25%
**标题:** Prompt-to-Gesture: Measuring the Capabilities of Image-to-Video Deictic Gesture Generation
**作者:** Hassan Ali, Doreen Jirak, Luca Müller
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.14953

**相关性说明:** This paper focuses on 2D gesture generation for gesture recognition (using image-to-video models to create synthetic training data), while the research profile is about 3D Human Motion Generation for Computer Graphics. The application domains are fundamentally different: this is about generating 2D video gestures for data augmentation in recognition tasks, not 3D motion generation for graphics. The paper doesn't address the pain points mentioned (3D reconstruction from 2D, VLM-based quality supervision, cross-modal alignment), and doesn't use the required methods (VLM, 3D motion generation model, feature filtering). While both involve "gesture," the goal and output are completely different - this is 2D video generation, not 3D motion generation.

**摘要:** 本文探讨了利用图像到视频生成模型创建合成手势数据的可行性，旨在缓解手势识别研究中的数据稀缺问题。通过设计一个基于提示的数据生成 pipeline，从少量人类参考样本生成逼真的指示性手势，并验证其在下游任务中的效果。
