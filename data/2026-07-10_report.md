## 📄 论文 1 - 相关性: 75%
**标题:** Ego-Human Motion Prediction with 3D-Aware LLM
**作者:** Yujin Bae, Jaewoo Jeong, Hyeonseong Kim
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.07001

**相关性说明:** This paper directly addresses the core pain points of bridging 2D visual information (first-person video, 3D scene features, point tracking) with 3D motion generation. It tackles temporal consistency by predicting future poses from past motion. The application domain is 3D human motion prediction from egocentric video, which matches the Computer Graphics/Vision field. However, it uses a 3D-aware LLM approach rather than the video generation models (Wanxiang, Seedance) or diffusion-based methods mentioned in the profile. The VLM-like quality control through language narration is a relevant approach. While highly relevant in application domain, the specific methodology differs from the profile's preferred methods.

**摘要:** Ego3DLM是一个用于从第一人称视角预测人体运动的框架，通过结合3D场景理解和跨模态 pose-language 联合预测来解决现有方法的局限性。该方法在 Nymeria 基准上实现了最先进的性能。


## 📄 论文 2 - 相关性: 45%
**标题:** Retrieving and Refining Winning Noise Tickets for Diffusion-Based Motion Generation
**作者:** Sakuya Ota, Qing Yu, Kent Fujiwara
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.06843

**相关性说明:** The paper addresses motion generation using diffusion models, which is in the same general field (Computer Graphics/Vision). However, the APPLICATION DOMAIN is fundamentally different: the paper focuses on TEXT-to-motion generation, while the research profile specifically requires 3D motion generation from 2D VIDEO priors. The profile mentions methods like Wanxiang/Seedance (video-to-motion) and visual feature extraction from video, but this paper uses text prompts as input. While there's some overlap in the broader theme of diffusion-based motion generation and addressing temporal consistency, this paper does not address the core pain points of bridging 2D visual information to 3D motion generation or video-based motion synthesis.

**摘要:** WINRO是一种无需训练、模型无关的框架，通过选择和优化"winning noise tickets"来改善扩散模型中文本到动作的对齐质量，解决语义漂移问题。


## 📄 论文 3 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** SpaR3D-MoE: Adaptive 3D Spatial Reasoning from Sparse Views Meets Geometry-Inductive Mixture-of-Experts
**作者:** Haida Feng, Hao Wei, Haolin Wang
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.06620

**相关性说明:** While this paper addresses 2D-to-3D understanding (bridging 2D semantic understanding and 3D spatial geometry), it focuses on 3D SPATIAL REASONING for VLM question-answering tasks (VSI-Bench, ScanQA, SQA3D), not 3D MOTION GENERATION. The application domain is fundamentally different: spatial reasoning/understanding vs. motion synthesis. The paper uses geometry-inductive MoE and adaptive sampling for scene understanding, whereas the research profile is about generating 3D motions from video priors with temporal consistency and physical plausibility. There's minimal overlap in methods - MoE for routing tokens vs. diffusion models for motion generation. Only the broad 2D-to-3D theme provides weak conceptual alignment.

**摘要:** SpaR3D-MoE是一种端到端框架,旨在通过仅使用稀疏RGB输入为多模态大语言模型赋予几何感知的自适应空间推理能力,解决2D语义理解与3D空间几何之间的表征差距问题。


## 📄 论文 4 - 相关性: 30%
**标题:** Geometric Collapse: When Vision Models Fail to Verify Physical Causality
**作者:** Wentao Zhang, Jinhu Qi, Weiqiang Jin
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.06871

**相关性说明:** This paper focuses on depth estimation and physical causality verification in vision models, which is a different application domain from 3D motion generation. While it touches on physical plausibility (one of the pain points), it doesn't address video-to-motion synthesis, temporal consistency in motion, or any of the specific methods mentioned (Wanxiang, Seedance, VLM for quality control in motion generation). The paper uses diffusion models only for depth estimation, not for motion generation. It studies a different task entirely - depth prediction rather than motion synthesis.

**摘要:** 本文提出Scrambled Edges方法，通过注入违反表面连续性、光照一致性和遮挡顺序的边缘线索来测试深度预测模型的物理合理性。研究发现当前的自监督学习方法在处理物理上不支持的边缘线索时存在系统性缺陷。
