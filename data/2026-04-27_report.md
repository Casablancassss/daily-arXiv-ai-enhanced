## 📄 论文 1 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** GenMatter: Perceiving Physical Objects with Generative Matter Models
**作者:** Eric Li, Arijit Dasgupta, Yoni Friedman
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.22160

**相关性说明:** The paper addresses motion-based object perception and scene understanding (grouping moving elements into coherent entities), which is fundamentally different from the research profile focused on VIDEO GENERATION from diffusion models and 2D-TO-3D MOTION GENERATION. While both are in Computer Vision, this paper deals with analysis/understanding of motion patterns rather than generation. It doesn't use diffusion models, VLMs, or video generation frameworks mentioned in the profile. The application domain mismatch is significant - this is about perceptual grouping for scene understanding, not about generating video priors or bridging 2D video to 3D motion generation.

**摘要:** 提出了一种受人类视觉感知启发的生成模型，用于运动场景的层次化分组感知。该模型将低层运动线索和高层外观特征分组为粒子，再将粒子分组为可独立运动的物理实体簇。通过并行化块Gibbs采样实现硬件加速推理，可在随机点、纹理和自然RGB视频等多种输入下工作。


## 📄 论文 2 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** Learning Reactive Human Motion Generation from Paired Interaction Data Using Transformer-Based Models
**作者:** Masato Soga, Ryuki Takebayashi
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.22164

**相关性说明:** While both papers are in the Computer Graphics / Computer Vision domain focusing on human motion, there is a significant mismatch. The research profile focuses on: (1) Diffusion-based video generation, (2) 2D video to 3D motion extraction, (3) VLM integration for quality control, and (4) quality control of generated video priors. The paper, however, uses Transformer-based models (not diffusion) for action-reaction motion generation from boxing videos. The paper does not address video priors, VLM integration, or diffusion-based generation - which are the core pain points in the profile. The application focus is also different: the profile deals with video generation models like Wanxiang/Seedance, while this paper focuses purely on human motion generation in interaction scenarios.

**摘要:** 该研究解决了交互场景下基于对方运动生成一人运动的问题，从拳击比赛视频中构建了成对的action-reaction运动序列数据集，比较了三种Transformer模型，并引入人物ID嵌入。实验表明简单Transformer表现最优，能够生成稳定的交互动作。

**标题:** PASR: Pose-Aware 3D Shape Retrieval from Occluded Single Views
**作者:** Jiaxin Shi, Guofeng Zhang, Wufei Ma
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.22658

**相关性说明:** This paper addresses 3D shape retrieval from single 2D views - a different application domain than the research profile which focuses on 3D motion generation from video.
