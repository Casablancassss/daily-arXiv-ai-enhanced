## 📄 论文 1 - 相关性: 50%
**标题:** Motion-Adapter: A Diffusion Model Adapter for Text-to-Motion Generation of Compound Actions
**作者:** Yue Jiang, Mingyu Yang, Liuyuxin Yang
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.16135

**相关性说明:** The paper addresses text-to-motion generation using diffusion models, which partially matches the "Text-to-Motion Synthesis" method in the profile. However, the key pain points in the research profile focus on (1) 2D video priors for 3D motion synthesis, (2) VLM-based quality control, and (3) visual guidance features extraction from 2D video - all of which are completely absent in this paper. The paper is purely text-to-motion without any visual/video guidance aspect. The core application is human motion generation, but the methodology gap is significant as it doesn't address the video-based or VLM-based quality control needs specified in the profile.

**摘要:** 本文提出Motion-Adapter模块，解决文本到动作生成中的复合动作合成难题。该模块通过解耦交叉注意力图作为结构掩码，引导扩散模型生成连贯的全身动作序列。


## 📄 论文 2 - 相关性: 25%
**标题:** CPU Optimization of a Monocular 3D Biomechanics Pipeline for Low-Resource Deployment
**作者:** Yan Zhang, Xiong Zhao
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.15665

**相关性说明:** The paper focuses on CPU optimization for monocular 3D biomechanics/motion capture systems, which is fundamentally different from the research profile's focus on text-to-motion generation and video generation models. While both deal with 3D human motion, this paper is about motion ANALYSIS/CAPTURE (estimating existing motions from video), NOT motion GENERATION (creating new motions from text). The methods used are also completely different - this paper uses system optimization, parallelization, and performance profiling, whereas the research profile asks for video generation models, VLMs, and text-to-motion synthesis. The application domain mismatch is significant: the researcher needs to GENERATE new motions, not CAPTURE existing motions.

**摘要:** 本文优化了MonocularBiomechanics单目3D生物力学流程，实现高效CPU-only执行。通过模型初始化重构、消除I/O序列化和改进CPU并行化，在AMD Ryzen 7 9700X上实现2.47倍吞吐量提升和59.6%运行时间减少，同时保持生物力学精度（偏差0.35°，r=0.998）。


## 📄 论文 3 - 相关性: 25%
**标题:** AHS: Adaptive Head Synthesis via Synthetic Data Augmentations
**作者:** Taewoong Kang, Hyojin Jang, Sohyun Jeong
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.15857

**相关性说明:** The paper focuses on 2D image-based head swapping/replacement (AHS - Adaptive Head Synthesis), which is a different application domain than 3D human motion generation. The research profile specifically needs methods for generating 3D human motions from text/video (Text-to-Motion Synthesis, 3D Motion Generation), while this paper deals with face replacement in 2D images using synthetic data augmentations. This is computer graphics but for image-based editing rather than 3D motion synthesis. The core pain points about 3D motion generation from text, video-to-3D motion synthesis, and VLM-based quality control are not addressed. Although both are in computer graphics, the application domain (2D head replacement vs 3D motion generation) is fundamentally different.

**摘要:** AHS是一种新型头部交换方法，通过合成数据增强策略解决自监督训练限制，能够处理完整上半身图像，在多样化的头部姿势、表情和发型场景中保持身份保真度和自然融合效果


## 📄 论文 4 - 相关性: 25%
**标题:** VEFX-Bench: A Holistic Benchmark for Generic Video Editing and Visual Effects
**作者:** Xiangbo Gao, Sicong Jiang, Bangya Liu
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.16272

**相关性说明:** This paper is about video editing quality evaluation benchmark (VEFX-Bench), while the research profile is about 3D HUMAN MOTION GENERATION from text/2D video. These are completely different application domains: one focuses on assessing video editing quality, the other on synthesizing 3D human motions. Although both use Vision Language Models (VLMs), the paper uses VLM for evaluating video editing outputs, not for quality control of generated 3D motions or extracting visual guidance features for motion synthesis. The paper addresses video editing assessment (a different task) rather than 3D motion synthesis from text or 2D video priors.

**摘要:** VEFX-Dataset是一个包含5049个视频编辑示例的人工标注数据集，涵盖9个主要编辑类别和32个子类别，标注三个解耦维度；VEFX-Reward是专门用于视频编辑质量评估的奖励模型，通过序数回归预测各维度质量分数；VEFX-Bench是包含300个精选视频-提示对的基准测试集


## 📄 论文 5 - 相关性: 25%
**标题:** MMGait: Towards Multi-Modal Gait Recognition
**作者:** Chenye Wang, Qingyuan Cai, Saihui Hou
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.15979

**相关性说明:** Despite being related to human motion, this paper addresses GAIT RECOGNITION (identifying people from their walking pattern), which is fundamentally different from the research profile's focus on 3D HUMAN MOTION GENERATION (synthesizing new motions from text/video). The paper deals with biometric identification using multiple sensors (RGB, depth, LiDAR), while the research profile needs motion synthesis methods. These are inverse tasks: recognition vs generation, and the application domain is computer vision/biometrics rather than computer graphics motion synthesis.

**摘要:** MMGait是一个综合性的多模态步态识别基准数据集，集成了来自5种异构传感器的数据，包含12种模态和334,060个序列。该工作提出了Omni Multi-Modal步态识别新任务，并设计了OmniGait基线方法在共享嵌入空间中统一单模态、跨模态和多模态识别范式。
