## 📄 论文 1 - 相关性: 55%
**标题:** RigPAPR: Rig-Based Animation of Static Neural Point Clouds from a Fixed-Viewpoint Video
**作者:** Shichong Peng, Yanshu Zhang, Ke Li
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.06685

**相关性说明:** Paper is in Computer Graphics domain and addresses relevant pain points (extracting 3D motion from 2D video, bridging 2D-to-3D). However, it uses traditional rigging/LBS methods (not diffusion-based video generation or VLM quality supervision). It's about animating static neural point clouds via rigging, not generating new 3D human motion from video using modern generative methods. Methodologically different from the research profile's focus on diffusion models and VLM-based video-to-motion generation.

**摘要:** 本文提出RigPAPR方法,旨在将静态神经点云(如高斯溅射)重建 animte为跟随单目固定视角驱动视频的动画,并恢复可重绑的3D资产。核心创新在于结合PAPR(近接注意力点渲染)技术与直接线性混合蒙皮(LBS),实现无网格代理、无姿态相关校正的自动rigging。


## 📄 论文 2 - 相关性: 55%
**标题:** DisPOSE: Projected Polystochastic Diffusion for Self-Supervised Multi-View 3D Human Pose Estimation
**作者:** Tony Danjun Wang, Tolga Birdal, Nassir Navab
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.07419

**相关性说明:** This paper addresses 3D human pose estimation from multi-view images using diffusion, while the research profile focuses on 3D human MOTION GENERATION (video synthesis). Although both deal with human motion, pose ESTIMATION (reconstructing from images) is fundamentally different from motion GENERATION (synthesizing new motions). The paper uses diffusion for a different application (pose estimation vs video generation), addresses different pain points (multi-person assignment vs temporal consistency in generated videos), and lacks alignment with VLM-based quality supervision. It's in the same general domain (human motion) but addresses a different core task.

**摘要:** DisPOSE是一种自监督框架，用于从多视角图像中恢复多个个体的3D人体姿态。它将离散的多人分配问题建模为生成扩散过程，通过可微的Sinkhorn投影学习有效的分配，并使用超图卷积解码器回归完整的3D骨架。该方法在标准数据集上优于现有自监督方法，并在手术室遮挡场景中表现优异，且仅用10%的伪标签就能保持99%的性能。


## 📄 论文 3 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** MotionEnhancer: Leveraging Video Diffusion for Motion-Enhanced Vision-Language Models
**作者:** Yifan Xu, Chao Zhang, Ruifei Ma
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.06853

**相关性说明:** While this paper uses related methods (Diffusion-based Video Generation and VLM), it addresses a different application domain. The paper focuses on enhancing VLM for "motion understanding" (analyzing/recognizing motion in videos), not 3D Human Motion Generation. The research profile requires generating synthesized 3D motions, not understanding motion from videos. The pain points about extracting 3D motion from 2D video and quality assessment are not addressed - this is about transferring knowledge from VDM to VLM for better motion comprehension, not bridging 2D visual features to 3D motion representations for generation. This is a cross-modal learning paper between VDM and VLM, not a video-to-motion or 3D motion generation paper.

**摘要:** 本文提出MotionEnhancer方法，利用视频扩散模型(VDM)的运动先验通过注意力对齐增强视觉语言模型(VLM)的细粒度运动理解能力，包含两个无参数模块MHS和MTTI，在无需额外训练或架构修改的情况下提升VLM在运动级视频理解任务上的性能。


## 📄 论文 4 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** Beyond Skeletons: Learning Animation Directly from Driving Videos with Same2X Training Strategy
**作者:** Yuan Zeng, Yujia Shi, Yuhao Yang
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.06903

**相关性说明:** This paper addresses human image animation (2D image-to-video) rather than 3D human motion generation. While it uses driving videos and diffusion transformers, the goal is animating static images with 2D video output, not extracting or generating 3D skeletal motion. The application domain is fundamentally different from the research profile's focus on 3D motion synthesis, temporal consistency in 3D motions, and bridging 2D visual features to 3D motion representations. The pain points are also mismatched - this paper doesn't address VLM-based quality assessment or cross-modal video-to-motion generation.

**摘要:** DirectAnimator是一种新的人类图像动画框架，它直接学习原始驱动视频，绕过姿态估计。通过引入Driving Cue Triplet（姿势、面部和位置线索）和CueFusion DiT块实现可靠控制，并采用Same2X训练策略解决跨ID学习问题。


## 📄 论文 5 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** AnchorWorld: Embodied Egocentric World Simulation with View-based Evolution Customization
**作者:** Yu Li, Menghan Xia, Gongye Liu
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.07326

**相关性说明:** This paper is about embodied egocentric world simulation with view-based customization, which is a different application domain from 3D Human Motion Generation. While it uses 3D human motion as an interaction modality for controlling world simulation, the core focus is on scene evolution and world modeling, not on generating or synthesizing 3D human motions themselves. The methods mentioned (anchor views, exocentric view supervision, text-controlled scene dynamics) are not aligned with the research profile's pain points (video-to-motion extraction, VLM quality assessment for generated videos, temporal consistency in 3D motion synthesis). This is more related to embodied AI/simulation research rather than computer graphics motion generation.

**摘要:** AnchorWorld是一个推进自我中心模拟的框架，通过3D人体运动交互和外源性视角监督实现增强的交互完整性，并提供灵活的自我演化世界定制机制。
