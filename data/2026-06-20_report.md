## 📄 论文 1 - 相关性: 45%
**标题:** CalTennis: Large Multi-View Tennis Video Dataset and Benchmark of Monocular-to-3D Pose Estimation
**作者:** Ilona Demler, Xinran Xie, Blake Werner
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.20542

**相关性说明:** This paper addresses monocular 3D pose estimation (recovering 3D human pose from single-view video), which is tangentially related to extracting 3D motion from 2D videos - one of the pain points. However, the application domain is fundamentally different: it's a sports analysis benchmark (tennis), not computer graphics or motion generation. The method (pose estimation) is also different from what's needed (motion generation models). While it partially touches on "2D to 3D" conversion, it doesn't address video generation quality, VLM-based quality supervision, or 3D motion generation for graphics applications.

**摘要:** Caltech网球数据集是一个大规模视频基准数据集，用于评估单目到3D姿态估计。该数据集包含超过1100万帧（51小时）的网球练习和比赛视频，来自40名球员，由2-6个同步相机以60Hz录制，比现有数据集大10倍，是第一个提供专家运动多视图录制的大规模基准。


## 📄 论文 2 - 相关性: 45%
**标题:** Current World Models Lack a Persistent State Core
**作者:** Jinpeng Lu, Dexu Zhu, Haoyuan Shi
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.20545

**相关性说明:** 该论文属于计算机图形学/视频生成领域，但研究重点是评估世界模型在观察之外的持续演化能力（persistent state），与研究档案的核心需求（从二维视频提取三维运动信息、三维动作生成）存在较大偏差。虽然都涉及视频生成，但该论文不关注3D运动提取或动作生成方法，也未涉及VLM质量监督或视觉引导特征提取等技术。

**摘要:** WRBench是首个系统性诊断基准，用于评估世界模型在摄像机不可见时是否继续演进。现有基准仅关注表面属性（保真度、运动可控性），忽视了世界状态在观察之外的持续演化能力。


## 📄 论文 3 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** LooseControlVideo: Directorial Video Control using Spatial Blocking
**作者:** Shariq Farooq Bhat, Niloy J. Mitra, Kalyan Sunkavalli
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.19495

**相关性说明:** 该论文使用Wan 2.2（万相）视频生成模型作为主干网络，这与研究Profile中的"视频生成模型（万相/Seedance）"方法有一定相关性。然而，两者的应用领域存在根本性差异：研究Profile关注的是如何从二维视频中提取三维运动信息，用于三维动作生成；而LooseControlVideo是关于文本到视频生成控制，重点是生成新的2D视频内容。虽然都涉及视频生成模型，但核心应用目标不同——一个是分析提取（2D→3D），另一个是内容生成（文本→2D视频）。因此属于"使用相似方法但应用领域不同"的情况。

**摘要:** LooseControlVideo是一种文本到视频生成框架，通过使用稀疏的定向3D边界框作为"阻挡"代理，实现直观且 expressive的空间控制，能够在复杂多对象场景中生成具有真实遮挡、动态和交互的视频。


## 📄 论文 4 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** ParaScale: Scale-Calibrated Camera-Motion Transfer via a Gauge-Invariant Parallax Number
**作者:** Zijie Meng
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.19805

**相关性说明:** This paper addresses camera motion transfer across different scene scales, which is a computer vision/geometry problem rather than video generation or 3D motion generation. While it deals with motion in videos, the core problem (scale calibration for camera motion transfer) does not match the research profile's pain points about video generation models, VLM quality supervision, or extracting 3D motion from 2D videos for graphics applications. The method is fundamentally different - it's about geometric scale invariance in camera motion, not about video generation or 3D motion synthesis.

**摘要:** 本文提出ParaScale模块，解决跨尺度相机运动迁移问题。核心发现是图像中的平移运动与深度成反比（||T||/Z），单目轨迹仅在深度尺度规约下有意义。由此引入无量纲的视差数Pi作为尺度不变的相机运动描述符，并开发了ParaScale模块在保持旋转的同时按帧重现实证视差。还提出新的度量PCE来暴露场景尺度不匹配问题。实验表明ParaScale在四个数量级的尺度范围内表现优异，PCE降低超过3倍且视觉质量无损。


## 📄 论文 5 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** SpatialSV: Internalizing Interpretable 3D Spatial Awareness in MLLMs via Task-Oriented Visual Supervision
**作者:** Jiayu Tang, Yuchen Zhou, Chao Gou
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.19915

**相关性说明:** The paper addresses 2D-to-3D conversion (depth maps, point clouds) which is methodologically related to extracting 3D information from 2D. However, the application domains are fundamentally different: the paper focuses on enhancing spatial awareness in Multimodal Large Language Models (general AI perception), while the research profile specifically targets 3D MOTION GENERATION for computer graphics. Motion generation requires temporal dynamics and kinematic constraints that are distinct from static spatial understanding tasks. This is similar to using the same "diffusion" method for image generation vs human motion generation - the application domains differ, making direct relevance low.

**摘要:** 本文提出SpatialSV框架,旨在让多模态大语言模型(MLLMs)内部化3D空间感知能力,同时提供可解释性。该方法通过任务导向的视觉监督,迫使模型将2D视觉特征主动提升为显式的3D表示(深度图、相机姿态、点云),从而实现可解释的空间知识学习。
