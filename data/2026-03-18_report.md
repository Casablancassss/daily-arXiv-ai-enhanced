## 📄 论文 1 - 相关性: 45%
**标题:** WorldCam: Interactive Autoregressive 3D Gaming Worlds with Camera Pose as a Unifying Geometric Representation
**作者:** Jisu Nam, Yicong Hong, Chun-Hao Paul Huang
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.16871

**相关性说明:** The paper is about game world generation and camera pose control for interactive 3D gaming - this is a DIFFERENT application domain from the research profile. The profile focuses on video generation models (Wanxiang/Seedance), extracting 3D motion from 2D video, cross-modal transfer from text→video→3D motion, and VLM-based quality assessment. While both are in Computer Graphics, this paper addresses interactive game world generation with camera control, not video generation or 3D motion generation. The methods (camera pose as spatial indexing, Lie algebra for 6-DOF poses) are domain-specific to game navigation rather than video/motion generation. Only the general area of Computer Graphics matches.

**摘要:** 本文提出CameraCTRL方法，通过将相机姿态作为统一的几何表示来解决交互式游戏世界模型中的动作控制和3D一致性问题。该方法使用基于物理的动作空间和李代数表示来推导精确的6自由度相机姿态，并将其作为空间索引实现长期导航中的几何一致性。实验表明该方法显著优于现有最先进方法。


## 📄 论文 2 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** VIGOR: VIdeo Geometry-Oriented Reward for Temporal Generative Alignment
**作者:** Tengjiao Yin, Jinglei Shi, Heng Guo
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.16271

**相关性说明:** While this paper addresses video generation quality control (one of the pain points), the application domain is fundamentally different. The research profile focuses on 3D MOTION generation and extraction from video, cross-modal transfer (text→video→3D MOTION), and ensuring physical plausibility in generated motions. VIGOR, however, focuses on geometric consistency in general video generation (addressing object deformation, spatial drift, depth issues) - NOT 3D motion generation or human motion synthesis. The paper uses diffusion models but for general video generation, not for 3D motion generation or human motion synthesis. The research profile mentions Wanxiang/Seedance specifically in the context of video generation for motion applications, but VIGOR is about general video quality assessment, not motion-specific quality control.

**摘要:** 提出了一种基于几何的视频扩散模型奖励模型，通过预训练几何基础模型评估多视图一致性，解决视频生成中的对象变形、空间漂移和深度违规等不一致伪影问题。


## 📄 论文 3 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** World Reconstruction From Inconsistent Views
**作者:** Lukas Höllein, Matthias Nießner
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.16736

**相关性说明:** This paper addresses 3D scene/world reconstruction from video diffusion models, which is a different application domain from the research profile's focus on 3D motion generation and extraction. While both deal with video-to-3D conversion, the profile specifically targets human/robot motion generation and quality control for video generation models (Wanxiang/Seedance), whereas this paper focuses on environment/scene reconstruction. The methods (geometric foundation models, point cloud alignment) differ from the profile's emphasis on VLM for quality supervision and cross-modal text→video→motion transfer. Only partially addresses the pain point of extracting 3D information from video, but for scene reconstruction rather than motion generation.

**摘要:** 提出一种新方法，通过非刚性对齐视频帧到全局一致坐标框架，从视频扩散模型生成高质量且3D一致的点云重建，实现可探索的3D环境生成


## 📄 论文 4 - 相关性: 30%
**标题:** GAP-MLLM: Geometry-Aligned Pre-training for Activating 3D Spatial Perception in Multimodal Large Language Models
**作者:** Jiaxin Zhang, Junjun Jiang, Haijie Li
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.16461

**相关性说明:** This paper focuses on 3D spatial perception in Multimodal LLMs for computer vision tasks (3D visual grounding, 3D dense captioning, 3D video object detection), while the research profile is in Computer Graphics focused on video generation, 3D motion extraction, and quality control. Although both involve VLMs and geometry, the application domains are different - this is computer vision/perception rather than computer graphics motion generation or video synthesis. The paper doesn't address video generation quality control, temporal consistency, or physical plausibility in generated motions which are key pain points in the profile.

**摘要:** 针对纯RGB输入的多模态大语言模型在3D空间感知方面的性能差距，提出GAP-MLLM几何对齐预训练范式，通过视觉提示联合任务和多层次渐进融合模块激活几何表示，在多个3D视觉任务上取得显著提升。


## 📄 论文 5 - 相关性: 30%
**标题:** Rethinking Pose Refinement in 3D Gaussian Splatting under Pose Prior and Geometric Uncertainty
**作者:** Mangyu Kong, Jaewon Lee, Seongwon Lee
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.16538

**相关性说明:** This paper focuses on 3D Gaussian Splatting camera pose refinement for scene localization - a different application domain than the research profile. While both are in Computer Graphics, the profile specifically targets video generation, 3D motion extraction from video, and quality control using VLM for models like Wanxiang/Seedance. This paper addresses pose estimation/localization problems, not video generation or motion synthesis. The methods (pose optimization, Fisher information, Monte Carlo sampling) are not related to video generation models or VLM-based quality assessment mentioned in the pain points.

**摘要:** 针对3DGS姿态细化中姿态和几何不确定性问题，提出结合蒙特卡洛姿态采样与费舍尔信息PnP优化的重定位框架，无需额外监督即可提高定位精度和稳定性。
