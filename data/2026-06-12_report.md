## 📄 论文 1 - 相关性: 55%
**标题:** TopoCap: Learning Topology-Agnostic Motion Priors for Monocular Video-to-Animation
**作者:** Cheng-Feng Pu, Jia-Peng Zhang, Meng-Hao Guo
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.12153

**相关性说明:** This paper addresses video-to-animation (2D video to 3D motion), which partially aligns with the research profile's pain point of "domain gap between 2D video and 3D motion generation." However, it uses Graph CVAE and flow matching instead of the specified methods (Video Generation Models like Wanxiang/Seedance, VLM for quality supervision). The paper is in the Computer Graphics domain but focuses on topology-agnostic motion retargeting rather than Text-to-Motion generation, and does not address visual guidance feature extraction or VLM-based quality filtering. The core method (Graph CVAE) differs from what the profile is looking for.

**摘要:** TopoCap是一个统一框架，能够从单目视频中提取运动并重定向到任意骨骼拓扑结构的角色（从双足动物到六足动物和无生命物体），无需测试时优化。核心思想是将运动视为连续低维流形，通过Graph CVAE学习通用运动流形，将异构运动链压缩为固定长度潜在码。


## 📄 论文 2 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** Making Foresight Actionable: Repurposing Representation Alignment in World Action Models
**作者:** Lu Qiu, Yizhuo Li, Yi Chen
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.12217

**相关性说明:** While the paper deals with video diffusion features and visual representation alignment, its application domain is ROBOTICS/World Action Models (WAMs) for robotic manipulation and control - NOT Computer Graphics/Text-to-Motion for human motion generation. Although both use "motion generation" in name, robot action control and human motion synthesis are fundamentally different applications. The paper addresses visual-to-action mismatch in robotic systems, not the 2D-to-3D motion recovery or VLM-based quality supervision for human motion synthesis as described in the research profile. Uses similar visual alignment methods but in completely wrong application domain.

**摘要:** 本文研究World Action Models (WAMs)中视觉未来生成与动作提取不匹配的问题，提出AGRA方法通过对齐视频扩散特征与视觉编码器表示来提升动作控制性能。


## 📄 论文 3 - 相关性: 25%
**标题:** LAST: Bridging Vision-Language and Action Manifolds via Gromov-Wasserstein Alignment
**作者:** Huaihai Lyu, Chaofan Chen, Yuheng Ji
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.11221

**相关性说明:** This paper addresses Vision-Language-Action (VLA) alignment for ROBOTICS control (robot manipulation), which is a fundamentally different application domain from Computer Graphics / Text-to-Motion for human motion generation. While both use Vision-Language Models, the goal here is robotic action space discretization (robot joint control), not human skeletal motion synthesis for graphics/animation. The pain points in the research profile focus on 2D video to 3D human motion recovery, VLM-based quality supervision for character animation, and visual guidance from video generation models - none of which are addressed by this robotics-focused paper. Using similar methods (VLM) in different application domains (robotics vs human motion graphics) should be considered irrelevant for this research profile.

**摘要:** 提出LAST方法，通过 Lie-algebraic Action Space Tokenizer 解决 VLA 学习中视觉-语言空间与动作空间之间的几何不兼容性问问题，实现动作流形与VL语义空间的度量对齐。

## 📄 论文 5 - 相关性: 25%
**标题:** 3D-CBM: A Framework for Concept-Based Interpretability in Generative 3D Modeling
**作者:** Ahmad Al-Kabbany
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.11446

**相关性说明:** The paper is about concept-based interpretability for static 3D shape generation (using PartNet/ShapeNet datasets), while the research profile is about Text-to-Motion generation - these are completely different application domains within Computer Graphics. The profile focuses on temporal motion synthesis from video/visual priors, while this paper deals with static 3D object/shape generation. Although both are in Computer Graphics, they address fundamentally different problems: motion generation (temporal) vs. shape generation (spatial). The methods are also unrelated - profile uses video generation models and VLM for motion synthesis, while this paper uses concept bottleneck models for interpretable 3D shape generation.

**摘要:** 研究提出了一种将概念瓶颈模型(CBM)融入3D生成架构的框架，用于解决深度几何学习中的"语义鸿沟"问题。该框架将原始几何输入(点云、网格)映射到可解释的原始特征和功能属性的多层次分类体系中，实现了语义可控的3D生成，并在测试时进行交互修正。
