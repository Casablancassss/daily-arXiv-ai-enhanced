# 📊 每日 arXiv 论文推荐

**研究画像:**
- 领域: Computer Graphics - Text-to-Motion
- 方法: Video Generation Models (Wan Xiang, Seedance), Vision Language Models (VLM), 3D Motion Generation Models, Visual Feature Extraction, Quality Assessment and Filtering
- 痛点: Extracting reliable 3D visual guidance features from 2D video priors, Ensuring VLM-based quality supervision aligns with motion fidelity, Cross-modal alignment between text, video, and 3D motion representations, Maintaining motion naturalness and temporal consistency in synthesis

**最相关的 5 篇论文:**


## 📄 论文 1 - 相关性: 75%
**标题:** AnimaSpark: A Feed-Forward Method for Animating Arbitrary 3D Objects
**作者:** Yiming Zhao, Haoyu Sun, Aoyu Wang
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.10988

**相关性说明:** The paper addresses Computer Graphics - Text-to-Motion with a pipeline that renders 3D models to images, uses video generation models (Seedance-like approach), and performs 2D-to-3D lifting - directly matching the research domain. It tackles key pain points: extracting visual features from 2D video priors, cross-modal alignment between text/video/3D motion, and maintaining motion naturalness. However, it lacks explicit VLM-based quality supervision mentioned in the profile, and the method uses a different approach (rendering static 3D to video) rather than pure text-to-motion generation. The application domain matches but the specific method differs from direct text-to-motion synthesis.

**摘要:** AnimaSpark是一种用于类别无关3D动画生成的新型流水线，通过将 rigged 静态3D模型渲染为多层图像表示，输入视频生成模型，然后使用关键点跟踪算法捕捉骨骼关节运动，最后将2D平面运动提升到3D空间来生成动画。


## 📄 论文 2 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** Making Time Editable in Video Diffusion Transformers
**作者:** Konstantin Kuklev, Viacheslav Vasilev, Alexander Kunitsyn
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.10183

**相关性说明:** The paper addresses temporal control in video generation (2D video output) while the research profile is about Text-to-Motion (3D human motion generation). Although both use diffusion transformers, they are in DIFFERENT application domains - video generation vs human motion generation. The paper touches on motion speed control conceptually but doesn't address the key pain points: extracting 3D visual guidance from 2D video priors, cross-modal alignment between text/video/3D motion, or VLM-based quality supervision for motion fidelity. The method is too far removed from the specific text-to-motion generation task.

**摘要:** 提出一种针对视频生成扩散Transformer的时间控制方法，通过添加轻量级时间模块实现对运动速度和 temporal结构的控制。


## 📄 论文 3 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** SCAIL-2: Unifying Controlled Character Animation with End-to-end In-Context Conditioning
**作者:** Wenhao Yan, Fengjia Guo, Zhuoyi Yang
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.10804

**相关性说明:** The paper is about Video-to-Motion/Character Animation (using driving video to animate reference character), while the research profile is about Text-to-Motion (generating 3D motion from text). These are different application domains. SCAIL-2 addresses video-driven character animation, not text-based motion generation. The pain points and methods also don't align - the profile focuses on VLM-based quality supervision and cross-modal text-video-motion alignment, while SCAIL-2 uses bias-aware DPO without text or VLM components. Both involve video and motion but serve fundamentally different purposes.

**摘要:** SCAIL-2是一个端到端的角色动画框架,直接使用驱动视频作为输入,无需中间表示(如姿态骨架或遮罩背景),避免信息丢失。为解决端到端训练数据不足的问题,构建了MotionPair-60K数据集,并提出偏置感知DPO方法减少合成数据差异。


## 📄 论文 4 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** WorldOlympiad: Can Your World Model Survive a Triathlon?
**作者:** Yuke Zhao, Wangbo Zhao, Weijie Wang
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.11129

**相关性说明:** This paper is about diagnosing/evaluating video world models (physical realism, geometric consistency, interaction fidelity) - which is a different application domain from Text-to-Motion. While it touches on geometric consistency and temporal aspects, it doesn't address the core Text-to-Motion task: generating 3D human motion from text/video. The paper uses similar methods (VLM, Gaussian splatting for geometry) but targets video quality assessment rather than motion generation. It's moderately relevant methodologically but addresses different pain points - more about evaluating video generation than synthesizing motion.

**摘要:** WorldOlympiad是一个用于诊断视频世界模型的新基准，评估物理真实性、几何一致性和交互保真度三个维度，涵盖游戏、机器人和真实世界视频场景。


## 📄 论文 5 - 相关性: 25%
**标题:** SD-GRPO: Verifiable Segment Decomposition for Long-Form Vision-Language Generation
**作者:** Hyunwoong Kim, Seongeun Lee, Hannah Yun
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.09871

**相关性说明:** Completely different application domain. The paper is about Vision-Language Model training/optimization (GRPO) for visual question answering, chart understanding, and image-grounded text generation tasks. This is NOT related to Text-to-Motion in Computer Graphics, which involves generating 3D human motion from text/video inputs. While the paper uses VLMs, it applies them to image understanding/captioning rather than motion generation or video-to-motion synthesis. The pain points are also entirely different - this paper addresses credit assignment in VLM training, not extracting 3D visual guidance from video priors or ensuring motion fidelity.

**摘要:** SD-GRPO通过将GRPO的单标量优势分解为按段落归一化的向量优势，解决视觉语言任务中粗粒度信用分配的问题。
