# 📊 每日 arXiv 论文推荐

**研究画像:**
- 领域: Computer Graphics / 3D Human Motion Generation
- 方法: Wanxiang (万相) video generation model, Seedance video generation model, Vision Language Model (VLM), Diffusion models for video generation, 3D motion generation models, Motion capture data extraction
- 痛点: Extracting reliable 3D visual guidance features from 2D video generated by diffusion models, How to effectively use VLM for quality supervision and filtering of video generation results, Bridging the domain gap between 2D video priors and 3D motion generation, Ensuring temporal consistency and motion quality in 3D motion synthesis

**最相关的 5 篇论文:**


## 📄 论文 1 - 相关性: 55%
**标题:** Beyond MoCap: Scaling Motion Tokenizers with Synthetic Human Motion for Generative Modeling
**作者:** Yiwen Yan, Wanning He, Yu-Wing Tai
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.27547

**相关性说明:** This paper is about 3D Human Motion Generation (matching the application domain), but it addresses a different problem - motion capture data diversity limitation through synthetic motion generation. The methods used (VQ-VAE tokenizer, MotionGPT) are different from the research profile's focus on 2D video generation (Wanxiang/Seedance) with VLM supervision and bridging 2D video priors to 3D motion. The paper doesn't address the core pain points: extracting 3D guidance from 2D video, using VLM for quality filtering, or bridging 2D-3D domain gaps. It focuses on expanding motion vocabulary through synthetic data rather than video-to-motion conversion pipelines.

**摘要:** 针对现有人体动作生成模型受限于动作捕捉数据集多样性不足的问题，提出利用大规模合成人体动作扩展动作表示空间的框架，结合重新设计的VQ-VAE分词器，实现更丰富的动作词汇覆盖。


## 📄 论文 2 - 相关性: 55%
**标题:** Long-Term Prediction of Local and Global Human Motion with Occlusion Recovery
**作者:** Qiaoyue Yang, Sven Heutger, Christopher Niemann
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.27900

**相关性说明:** The paper IS about 3D Human Motion Generation (same application domain), but it addresses a different problem - direct motion prediction using Transformers rather than the specific pipeline the profile needs: extracting 3D motion from diffusion-generated video, using VLM for supervision/filtering, or bridging 2D video priors to 3D motion. The paper focuses on occlusion recovery and temporal prediction with Transformers, not the 2D-to-3D video-to-motion pipeline with diffusion models and VLMs that the pain points describe.

**摘要:** 本文提出一种基于非自回归Transformer的人体全身运动预测方法，能够同时预测局部姿态和全局运动，并处理遮挡导致的缺失关节问题。


## 📄 论文 3 - 相关性: 45%
**标题:** HumanMoveVQA: Can Video MLLMs reason about human movement in videos?
**作者:** Pulkit Gera, Faegheh Sardari, Asmar Nadeem
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.27999

**相关性说明:** 该论文与研究profile存在一定关联但应用领域不同。论文研究的是利用Video MLLMs理解和推理视频中的人类运动（分析已有视频），而研究profile是关于3D人类运动生成（创建新 motion）。两者都涉及从2D视频提取3D运动信息，这契合pain point 1和3（从2D视频提取3D特征、 bridging domain gap）。但该论文是关于运动理解/分析，不是运动生成。论文未涉及diffusion视频生成、Wanxiang/Seedance模型、也没有解决视频生成质量监督过滤问题。核心方法是用VQA评估Video MLLM能力，而非3D motion generation model。这是related but different application domain - 运动理解vs运动生成。

**摘要:** 本文提出HumanMoveVQA，首个评估多模态大语言模型全局人类运动（轨迹和方向变化）推理能力的基准测试，解决现有模型将复杂人体运动简化为粗语义标签的问题。


## 📄 论文 4 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** Perceptual 3D Simulation With Physical World Modeling
**作者:** Wanhee Lee, Klemen Kotar, Rahul Mysore Venkatesh
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.27575

**相关性说明:** While this paper deals with 3D scene prediction and physical world modeling, it is NOT in the Computer Graphics / 3D Human Motion Generation domain. The research profile specifically focuses on extracting 3D human motion from 2D video generated by diffusion models (Wanxiang, Seedance), using VLM for filtering, and bridging 2D video-to-3D motion. This paper addresses general 3D scene prediction, object manipulation, and view synthesis - not human motion generation. The application domain is fundamentally different (scene understanding vs human motion synthesis). It uses some related concepts (3D transformation signals, partial observations) but does not address the pain points of video-to-motion generation, temporal consistency in human motion, or motion quality supervision.

**摘要:** P3Sim是一个物理世界建模系统，用于在部分观察和不完整3D变换信号下模拟未来场景状态。该系统由三个交互组件构成：学习到的物理世界模型、几何 conditioning 模块和持久场景记忆。


## 📄 论文 5 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** Scene and Human in One World: Reconstruction in a Feedforward Pass
**作者:** Boao Shi, Qiao Feng, Yiming Huang
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.27720

**相关性说明:** The paper addresses 3D human MESH RECONSTRUCTION from monocular video, which is a different application domain from the research profile's 3D HUMAN MOTION GENERATION. While both deal with human motion/mesh in computer graphics, reconstruction (recovering existing pose from video) is fundamentally different from motion generation (synthesizing new motion sequences). The paper uses parametric human models (SMPL) and scene geometry constraints - not diffusion models, video generation models, or motion synthesis methods mentioned in the profile. Only loosely related in that both are in the broader computer graphics/human analysis area.

**摘要:** SHOW是一个统一的度量空间人体网格恢复框架,通过将参数化人体模型与3D场景重建相结合,解决单目相机中的人体尺度歧义、人体-场景对齐和遮挡干扰问题。该方法利用人体语义和尺度先验注入到归一化点图预测中,同时利用场景几何约束人体网格估计,并引入提示性掩码机制处理复杂多人场景。
