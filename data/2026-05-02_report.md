## 📄 论文 1 - 相关性: 55%
**标题:** MoCapAnything V2: End-to-End Motion Capture for Arbitrary Skeletons
**作者:** Kehong Gong, Zhengyu Wen, Dao Thien Phong
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.28130

**相关性说明:** The paper addresses "Video-to-Motion" (extracting 3D motion from video), while the research profile focuses on "Text-to-Motion" (generating 3D motion from text). Although both deal with 3D motion generation and share some methodological similarities (bridging 2D visual representations to 3D motion output), the input modality is fundamentally different. The paper addresses pain points #1 (extracting visual features from 2D video) and #3 (bridging 2D→3D domain gap), but does not address text-based motion generation or diffusion-based motion synthesis, which are key to the research profile. It's in the same general Computer Graphics domain but different input-output paradigm.

**摘要:** 本文提出首个端到端的任意骨骼动作捕捉框架，将Video-to-Pose和Pose-to-Rotation统一为可学习模块，通过引入参考pose-rotation对解决姿态到旋转的歧义问题，实现从单目视频直接预测关节旋转。


## 📄 论文 2 - 相关性: 50%
**标题:** Uni-HOI:A Unified framework for Learning the Joint distribution of Text and Human-Object Interaction
**作者:** Mengfei Zhang, Jinlu Zhang, Zhigang Tu
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.27491

**相关性说明:** The paper focuses on text-to-motion for human-object interaction using LLM + VQ-VAE, which is a different approach from the research profile's emphasis on visual guidance from 2D video sequences, VLM-based visual feature extraction, and video generation models (Wanxiang,Seedance). The pain points specifically mention bridging 2D visual representations to 3D motion output, but Uni-HOI uses text as the primary input modality, not visual/video inputs. The application domain is text-to-motion but the methodology (LLM+VQ-VAE vs VLM+visual features from video) doesn't match the specific methods and pain points outlined.

**摘要:** Uni-HOI是一个统一框架，利用大语言模型(LLM)和两个运动专用VQ-VAE来学习文本、人类运动和物体运动的联合分布，通过两阶段训练策略实现多种HOI相关任务。


## 📄 论文 3 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** REVIVE 3D: Refinement via Encoded Voluminous Inflated prior for Volume Enhancement
**作者:** Hankyeol Lee, Wooyeol Baek, Seongdo Kim
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.27504

**相关性说明:** This paper focuses on 3D asset/object generation from 2D flattened images (volume enhancement), while the research profile is specifically about Text-to-Motion generation (generating 3D human motion). These are different application domains in computer graphics - object generation vs motion generation. Although both use diffusion-based methods, the target output is fundamentally different (static 3D assets vs temporal human motion sequences). The pain points don't align: the paper addresses geometric volume quality, not temporal consistency or motion smoothness.

**摘要:** REVIVE 3D是一个从2D flatten images生成voluminous 3D assets的两阶段框架，包含Inflation Prior构建和3D Latent Refinement两个阶段，并提出Compactness和Normal Anisotropy指标进行评估。实验表明该方法在挑战性数据集上达到最先进的性能。


## 📄 论文 4 - 相关性: 25%
**标题:** YOSE: You Only Select Essential Tokens for Efficient DiT-based Video Object Removal
**作者:** Chenyang Wu, Lina Lei, Fan Li
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.27322

**相关性说明:** This paper focuses on video object removal (video editing/inpainting) using DiT-based diffusion models, which is a completely different application domain from Text-to-Motion (3D human motion generation from visual/text guidance). While both use diffusion models, the paper addresses 2D video manipulation rather than bridging 2D visual representations to 3D motion output. The pain points are also different - this paper targets inference acceleration for video editing, not motion quality, temporal consistency in motion generation, or visual guidance feature extraction for motion synthesis. Therefore, it only shares methodological similarity (diffusion models) but fails to address any of the research profile's specific pain points or application domains.

**摘要:** 本文提出YOSE框架，通过Batch Variable-length Indexing (BVI)和Diffusion Process Simulator (DiffSim)模块实现视频对象移除的高效推理加速，在保持与基线相当视觉质量的同时，实现高达2.5倍的推理速度提升。


## 📄 论文 5 - 相关性: 25%
**标题:** Generate Your Talking Avatar from Video Reference
**作者:** Zujin Guo, Zhenhui Ye, Yi Ren
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.27918

**相关性说明:** This paper is about "talking avatar" video generation (2D visual content), not 3D motion generation. The research profile specifically targets Text-to-Motion in Computer Graphics - generating 3D human motion data. Talking avatar focuses on facial animation/lip-sync for video output, which is fundamentally different from motion synthesis. While both use video understanding, this paper generates 2D video content rather than bridging 2D visual representations to 3D motion output. The pain points are completely different - one deals with identity preservation in video generation, the other with extracting visual guidance for 3D motion and ensuring temporal consistency in motion data.

**摘要:** TAVR（Talking Avatar from Video Reference）是一个利用跨场景视频输入生成说话头像的新框架，通过token选择模块和三阶段训练方案解决了传统方法受限于单一视角和静态图像的问题。
