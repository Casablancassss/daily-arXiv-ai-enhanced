- 领域: Computer Graphics / Computer Vision
- 方法: Vision Language Models (VLM), Video Generation Models (Wanxiang, Seedance), Visual Feature Extraction, Diffusion-based Motion Generation, Text-to-Motion Synthesis
- 痛点: Bridging 2D video priors to 3D motion space with reliable feature extraction, Ensuring quality supervision and filtering of generated video content, Maintaining temporal consistency and motion realism in 3D synthesis, Reducing semantic gap between visual features and motion semantics

## 📄 论文 1 - 相关性: 30%
**标题:** SceneAssistant: A Visual Feedback Agent for Open-Vocabulary 3D Scene Generation
**作者:** Jun Luo, Jiaxiang Tang, Ruijie Lu
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.12238

**相关性说明:** The paper focuses on 3D SCENE generation (static spatial composition of objects), while the research profile is about 3D MOTION generation and VIDEO synthesis with temporal consistency. These are fundamentally different application domains: motion/video generation involves dynamic temporal sequences, whereas scene generation involves static spatial arrangements. While both use VLM, the paper does not address motion realism, temporal consistency, text-to-motion synthesis, or diffusion-based motion generation - all core to the research profile. The pain points in the profile specifically mention "motion space," "temporal consistency," and "motion realism" which are absent in this scene generation paper.

**摘要:** SceneAssistant是一个基于视觉反馈的智能体，用于从自然语言生成开放词汇的3D场景。它结合了现代3D对象生成模型与视觉语言模型的空间推理和规划能力，通过原子操作迭代优化场景布局。


## 📄 论文 2 - 相关性: 30%
**标题:** DreamVideo-Omni: Omni-Motion Controlled Multi-Subject Video Customization with Latent Identity Reinforcement Learning
**作者:** Yujie Wei, Xinyu Liu, Shiwei Zhang
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.12257

**相关性说明:** The paper focuses on 2D VIDEO generation with multi-subject control, while the research profile is about 3D MOTION GENERATION and text-to-motion synthesis. Although both use diffusion models, they target fundamentally different application domains: 2D video synthesis vs 3D human/robot motion generation. The paper addresses 2D video identity preservation and motion control, not bridging 2D video priors to 3D motion space, temporal consistency in 3D synthesis, or text-to-3D-motion generation - which are the core pain points in the research profile. This is similar to comparing diffusion for image generation vs diffusion for human motion generation - they use similar methods but address completely different problems.

**摘要:** DreamVideo-Omni是一个统一框架，通过渐进式两阶段训练实现多主体定制和全方位运动控制。它整合了条件感知3D旋转位置嵌入、分层运动注入策略、群体和角色嵌入来解决多主体歧义问题，并通过潜在身份奖励反馈学习来缓解身份退化问题。


## 📄 论文 3 - 相关性: 30%
**标题:** EVATok: Adaptive Length Video Tokenization for Efficient Visual Autoregressive Generation
**作者:** Tianwei Xiong, Jun Hao Liew, Zilong Huang
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.12267

**相关性说明:** This paper is about 2D video tokenization and autoregressive video generation, while the research profile focuses on 3D motion generation, text-to-motion synthesis, and bridging 2D video priors to 3D motion space. The application domains are fundamentally different: the profile seeks methods for human motion generation in 3D, while EVATok addresses 2D video reconstruction and generation efficiency. Although both are in computer vision, the specific application (2D video vs 3D motion synthesis) does not match. The paper uses autoregressive generation rather than diffusion-based motion generation mentioned in the profile.

**摘要:** EVATok是一种高效视频自适应tokenizer框架，通过根据视频内容复杂度动态分配token来解决传统tokenizer的均匀分配问题，实现了24.4%以上的token节省，同时保持甚至提升了视频重建质量和生成效果。


## 📄 论文 4 - 相关性: 25%
**标题:** EgoIntent: An Egocentric Step-level Benchmark for Understanding What, Why, and Next
**作者:** Ye Pan, Chi Kit Wong, Yuanhuiyi Lyu
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.12147

**相关性说明:** This paper focuses on egocentric video understanding and intent recognition (analyzing/vision tasks), while the research profile is about computer graphics focused on video/motion generation (synthesis tasks). The application domains are fundamentally different: video understanding vs. video generation. While both involve VLMs, the paper uses them for intent recognition in videos rather than for generating 3D motion or video content. The pain points in the profile relate to bridging 2D-to-3D, temporal consistency in synthesis, and diffusion-based motion generation - none of which are addressed by this benchmark paper on intent understanding.

**摘要:** EgoIntent是一个用于评估自我中心视频中步骤级意图理解能力的基准数据集，包含3,014个步骤和15个日常场景，从本地意图(What)、全局意图(Why)和下一步计划(Next)三个维度评估多模态大语言模型。


## 📄 论文 5 - 相关性: 25%
**标题:** Real-World Point Tracking with Verifier-Guided Pseudo-Labeling
**作者:** Görkay Aydemir, Fatma Güney, Weidi Xie
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.12217

**相关性说明:** This paper focuses on 2D point tracking with verifier-guided pseudo-labeling for long videos, which is a different application domain from the research profile's focus on 3D motion generation, text-to-motion synthesis, and video generation. While both are in Computer Vision, the paper addresses point tracking (estimating 2D trajectories of points) rather than 3D motion synthesis or diffusion-based motion generation. The methods used (point tracking, pseudo-labeling) are not aligned with the profile's methods (VLM, diffusion models, video generation models like Wanxiang/Seedance). The paper touches on quality supervision/pseudo-label quality control which is tangentially related to one pain point, but the core application (2D point tracking) is fundamentally different from 3D motion generation.

**摘要:** 本文提出Verifier元模型，用于评估跟踪器预测的可靠性并指导伪标签生成，以解决长视频点跟踪模型在真实场景中性能下降的问题。
