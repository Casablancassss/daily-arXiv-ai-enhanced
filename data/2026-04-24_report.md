## 📄 论文 1 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** Exploring the Role of Synthetic Data Augmentation in Controllable Human-Centric Video Generation
**作者:** Yuanchen Fei, Yude Zou, Zejian Kang
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.21291

**相关性说明:** The paper focuses on 2D human video generation using synthetic data augmentation, while the research profile is specifically about 3D motion generation from 2D video features. This is a fundamental mismatch in application domain - the paper addresses generating human videos (2D→2D or text→2D), not extracting 3D motion from video (2D→3D). While both use diffusion models and mention temporal consistency, the core objective is different: video synthesis vs. 3D motion generation. The pain points about bridging 2D-3D domain gaps and 3D motion generation are not addressed.

**摘要:** 本文系统研究了合成数据在可控人类视频生成中的作用，提出了一种基于扩散模型的框架，允许对外观和动作进行细粒度控制，并提供了一个统一的测试平台来分析合成数据与真实数据在训练过程中的交互方式。


## 📄 论文 2 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** Sapiens2
**作者:** Rawal Khirodkar, He Wen, Julieta Martinez
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.21681

**相关性说明:** Sapiens2 focuses on 2D human-centric vision tasks (pose estimation, segmentation, normal estimation) using Transformers, while the research profile is about 3D motion generation from 2D video using diffusion models and video generation models. Although pose estimation is listed in both, Sapiens2 addresses 2D perception tasks rather than 3D motion generation or video synthesis. The application domain is fundamentally different - this is about understanding/estimating 2D human pose, not generating 3D motion from video. It could serve as a preprocessing step but doesn't directly address the pain points of bridging 2D video to 3D motion generation, temporal consistency, or physical plausibility.

**摘要:** Sapiens2是一个用于人体中心视觉的高分辨率Transformer模型系列，参数量从0.4B到5B，支持原生1K分辨率和最高4K分辨率的分层变体，通过结合掩码图像重建与自蒸馏对比学习，在10亿张高质量人类图像上预训练，在姿态估计、身体部位分割、法线估计等任务上达到新的最先进的性能。


## 📄 论文 3 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** Discriminative-Generative Synergy for Occlusion Robust 3D Human Mesh Recovery
**作者:** Yang Liu, Zhiyong Zhang
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.21712

**相关性说明:** This paper addresses 3D human mesh recovery from SINGLE RGB images, while the research profile focuses on 2D VIDEO to 3D MOTION generation (temporal motion synthesis). The key mismatch: (1) This is static single-image pose estimation, not video-based motion generation; (2) It doesn't address temporal consistency which is a major pain point in the profile; (3) It's about mesh recovery, not motion generation. While both involve diffusion models and human motion/pose, the application domains are fundamentally different - static pose estimation vs. temporal motion synthesis from video. The paper uses similar methods (ViT + diffusion) but in a different application context.

**摘要:** 本文提出了一种脑启发的协同框架，将ViT的判别能力与条件扩散模型的生成能力相结合，用于从单目RGB图像恢复3D人体网格，特别是在部分或严重遮挡情况下。框架包含多样性一致性特征学习模块和跨注意力多层次融合机制，在标准基准上实现了优越性能。


## 📄 论文 4 - 相关性: 35%

**作者:** Guangkai Xu, Hua Geng, Huanyi Zheng
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.21713

**相关性说明:** This paper focuses on 3D geometry estimation (depth, camera pose, point cloud reconstruction) for scene understanding, while the research profile is about 3D motion generation from video for character animation/synthesis. Although both are in Computer Vision/Graphics and deal with 2D-to-3D conversion, the application domains are fundamentally different. The paper addresses geometric reconstruction, not human motion synthesis or generation.

**摘要:** 本文研究了视觉几何估计中的关键性能因素，提出了CARVE模型，通过一致性损失函数和高分辨率设计提升性能。


## 📄 论文 5 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** Grounding Video Reasoning in Physical Signals
**作者:** Alibay Osmanli, Zixu Cheng, Shaogang Gong
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.21873

**相关性说明:** This paper focuses on physical video understanding/benchmark evaluation (VQA task), NOT 2D video to 3D motion generation. While the research profile deals with generating 3D motion from 2D video and ensuring physical plausibility in synthesized motions, this paper is about evaluating VLMs' reasoning capabilities on understanding physical events in videos. The application domain is completely different - video understanding/reasoning vs motion generation/synthesis. Only tangential relevance through shared interest in "physical plausibility" but this is about reasoning about physical events in video, not generating physically plausible motions.

**摘要:** 本文提出了一个针对物理视频理解的定位基准测试，通过扩展V-STaR的多维度评估框架，对四个视频源、六种物理领域、三种提示家族和四种输入条件进行系统评估，发现物理提示整体表现最佳、空间定位能力最弱，提示鲁棒性具有选择性，强调视频Q&A基准测试需要报告物理定位、提示感知和扰动感知的诊断信息。
