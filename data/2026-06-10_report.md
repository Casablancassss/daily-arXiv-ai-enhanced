# 📊 每日 arXiv 论文推荐

- 领域: Computer Graphics - Text-to-Motion Generation
- 方法: Video Generation Models (万相, Seedance), Vision-Language Models (VLM), Visual Feature Extraction, 3D Motion Generation Models
- 痛点: Extracting reliable visual guidance features from 2D video outputs, Ensuring quality consistency between video prior and 3D motion generation, Bridging the gap between 2D visual representations and 3D motion space

**最相关的 5 篇论文:**


## 📄 论文 1 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** TBD-VLA: Temporal Block Diffusion Vision Language Action Model
**作者:** Sung-Wook Lee, Xuhui Kang, Yen-Ling Kuo
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.07895

**相关性说明:** This paper addresses Vision-Language-Action (VLA) for ROBOT MANIPULATION, which is a different application domain from Computer Graphics - Text-to-Motion Generation. The research profile focuses on generating human motion for graphics (bridging 2D video to 3D human motion), while this paper focuses on robot control actions. Both use diffusion methods but in fundamentally different domains: robot manipulation vs human motion synthesis. The paper does not address the pain points of extracting visual guidance from video or ensuring quality consistency between video prior and 3D motion generation.

**摘要:** TBD-VLA是一种新的离散视觉-语言-动作(VLA)框架，通过结合块扩散(block diffusion)实现时序动作生成，兼顾时序连贯性和推理速度。


## 📄 论文 2 - 相关性: 30%
**标题:** ViMax: Agentic Video Generation
**作者:** Lingxuan Huang, Sizhe He, Hengji Zhou
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.07649

**相关性说明:** The paper is about VIDEO GENERATION (2D video content creation), while the research profile is about TEXT-TO-MOTION GENERATION (3D human motion generation from 2D inputs). Although both use Video Generation Models and VLMs, the application domains are fundamentally different. The paper addresses visual consistency in 2D video generation, not bridging 2D video to 3D motion space. This is a case of similar methods used in wrong application domain - video generation for entertainment vs. motion generation for computer graphics.

**摘要:** ViMax是一个基于多智能体协作的长视频生成框架，通过分层叙事引擎、检索增强生成和依赖感知的视觉一致性机制，解决现有短片段方法缺乏叙事结构和视觉一致性的问题，实现多场景时间线上的故事完整性和视觉连贯性。


## 📄 论文 3 - 相关性: 30%
**标题:** What Makes Video World Model Latents Action-Relevant: Prediction over Reconstruction
**作者:** Jewon Yeom, Hanseul Kim, Jeongjae Park
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.07687

**相关性说明:** This paper studies video world models for understanding action-related representations in videos (action recognition/understanding), NOT for text-to-3D-motion generation. While it discusses video pretraining and visual features, the application domain is fundamentally different - it's about analyzing what makes video latents action-relevant for video understanding, not generating 3D human motion. The paper focuses on pixel reconstruction vs temporal prediction for action recoverability in videos, which is unrelated to the pain points of bridging 2D video to 3D motion generation for computer graphics.

**摘要:** 研究视频世界模型中哪些预训练信号在潜在空间中诱导动作相关结构。通过统一探测评估发现，时间视频预训练而非像素重构保真度是动作可恢复性的主要驱动因素，视频预训练自监督编码器在视觉保真度和动作预测间实现最佳Pareto权衡。


## 📄 论文 4 - 相关性: 30%
**标题:** Late-Layer Fusion is Enough: Dual-Path Vision Token Routing for Multimodal Large Language Models under Visual Saturation
**作者:** Siyuan Liu, Jinyang Wu
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.09131

**相关性说明:** The paper addresses computational efficiency in Vision-Language Models (token routing, late-layer fusion), which is methodologically different from the core research need. While both involve VLMs, this paper focuses on optimizing multimodal LLM efficiency for general visual understanding tasks, NOT on bridging 2D video features to 3D motion generation. The pain points are completely different: this paper tackles visual-text token computation mismatch, while the research profile needs 2D-to-3D cross-domain feature extraction and motion synthesis. This is a case of using similar high-level methods (VLMs) in fundamentally different application domains.

**摘要:** 本文提出DPVR（双路径视觉token路由）框架，解决多模态大语言模型中视觉和文本token计算效率不匹配的问题。通过在中间层将视觉token路由到旁路分支，仅用约3%可训练参数实现延迟融合，显著减少深层Transformer中的视觉计算量。


## 📄 论文 5 - 相关性: 30%
**标题:** MaskAlign: Token-Subset Representation Alignment for Efficient Diffusion Training
**作者:** Lianyu Pang, Tianlin Pan, Cheng Da
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.08788

**相关性说明:** This paper addresses representation alignment in diffusion models for IMAGE generation, not text-to-motion generation. While it uses diffusion models (similar method), the application domain is completely different - it's about improving image generation quality, not generating 3D human motion from text. The pain points are unrelated: this paper focuses on noisy-to-clean representation alignment in image diffusion, whereas the research profile deals with bridging 2D video outputs to 3D motion space. These are different application domains even though both involve diffusion models.

**摘要:** 本文提出MaskAlign方法，通过标记子集表示对齐来解决扩散变换器训练中干净图像特征与噪声输入之间的表示不对齐问题，并引入预掩码标记混合块减少信息损失。
