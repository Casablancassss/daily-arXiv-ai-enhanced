## 📄 论文 1 - 相关性: 78%
**标题:** AnyAct: Towards Human Reenactment of Character Motion From Video
**作者:** Liuhan Chen, Lei Zhong, Jiewei Wang
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.15497

**相关性说明:** This paper directly addresses Computer Graphics / Human Motion Generation with strong alignment to multiple pain points. It tackles: (1) 3D structure recovery from 2D video via unsupervised 3D-2D projection, (2) Quality degradation when transferring 2D video priors to 3D motion domains through progressive training strategy, and (3) Temporal coherence issues in motion synthesis via global-local motion decoupling. While Text-to-Motion methods aren't involved, the core application domain of human motion generation and motion retargeting matches perfectly. The paper's focus on extracting reliable visual guidance features from character videos and controlling 3D motion aligns with the "Visual-guided Motion Control" method mentioned in the profile.

**摘要:** 本文提出AnyAct，解决从非人类角色单目视频直接生成人类动作表演的问题。通过分析发现sparse local articulated motion cues可跨越large structural differences提供stable bridge。核心创新包括：无监督3D-2D投影、progressive训练策略、global-local运动解耦。为motion retargeting开辟新方向。


## 📄 论文 2 - 相关性: 38%
**标题:** Unsupervised 3D Human Pose Estimation via Conditional Multi-view Ancestral Sampling
**作者:** Ryohei Goto, Takuya Fujihashi, Shunsuke Saruwatari
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.15583

**相关性说明:** This paper focuses on 3D human pose ESTIMATION (recovering pose from images), while the research profile is about Human Motion GENERATION (synthesizing/creating new motion). While both deal with human pose conceptually, they are inverse problems - estimation vs. synthesis. The paper shares some methodological elements (diffusion models, motion priors) and addresses geometric consistency in 3D reconstruction, which partially aligns with Pain Point #1. However, it does not address text-to-motion, video-guided motion control, temporal coherence in motion synthesis, or any generation-specific challenges in the research profile.

**摘要:** 本文提出利用预训练运动扩散模型的2D扩散先验，通过条件多视角祖先采样(cMAS)实现单目图像的3D人体姿态估计，无需3D监督即可在跨域场景和极端姿态下取得优异性能。


## 📄 论文 3 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** EgoExo-WM: Unlocking Exo Video for Ego World Models
**作者:** Danny Tran, Roberto Martín-Martín, Kristen Grauman
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.15477

**相关性说明:** While this paper involves human pose extraction and ego-exo view transformation, its primary application domain is Robotics/AI world models rather than Computer Graphics human motion generation. The paper focuses on view transformation and prediction for planning, not on synthesizing new human motions or addressing CG-specific pain points like diffusion-based motion synthesis, temporal coherence in motion, or multi-modal motion generation. The human pose is used as a conditioning signal for world models, not as output to generate. This is a different application domain (robotics/vision AI vs. computer graphics motion synthesis).

**摘要:** 本文提出一种桥接外视角视频与自我视角视频的方法，通过提取人体姿态并结合运动学先验进行视角转换，使野外视频可用于训练自我中心世界模型，从而提升预测和规划能力。


## 📄 论文 4 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** Registers Matter for Pixel-Space Diffusion Transformers
**作者:** Nikita Starodubcev, Ilia Sudakov, Ilya Drobyshevskiy
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.16147

**相关性说明:** While this paper discusses pixel-space diffusion transformers (which share some architectural similarities with the video generation models mentioned in the profile like Seedance/Wanxiang), the application domain is fundamentally different. The paper focuses on improving image/video synthesis quality through register tokens—a general computer vision/graphics concern. It does NOT address any of the specific pain points related to human motion generation: 3D structure recovery from 2D video, cross-modal textual-motion alignment, visual guidance for motion control, or temporal coherence in synthesized motions. The research profile specifically requires diffusion-based methods tailored for human motion generation, not image generation.

**摘要:** Register tokens improve pixel-space Diffusion Transformers despite DiTs not having the patch-token outlier problem that affects Vision Transformers—suggesting a distinct beneficial mechanism beyond outlier mitigation.


## 📄 论文 5 - 相关性: 25%
**标题:** Deep Pre-Alignment for VLMs
**作者:** Tianyu Yu, Kechen Fang, Zihao Wan
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.15300

**相关性说明:** This paper focuses on improving Vision Language Model (VLM) architectures for general multi-modal understanding (benchmarks like MMMU, MathVista). While it shares the "VLM" keyword with the research profile (which lists "VLM for Quality Supervision"), the application domains are completely different: this paper targets VLM training/alignment for general AI benchmarks, while the research profile seeks VLM-guided techniques for human motion generation in computer graphics. The paper does not address any of the specific pain points around 3D geometry recovery, motion synthesis, or temporal coherence. The method (small VLM as perceiver) solves VLM internal alignment, not motion-domain challenges.

**摘要:** 本文提出DPA架构，通过用小型VLM替代传统ViT编码器作为预对齐感知器，解决VLM中视觉特征与文本空间的深层对齐问题，在4B-32B参数规模的多模态基准测试中显著优于现有方法，同时大幅减少语言能力遗忘。核心创新在于将对齐任务前置到感知器阶段，使主LLM能专注于深层推理。
