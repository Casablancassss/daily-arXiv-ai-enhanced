## 📄 论文 1 - 相关性: 30%
**标题:** AHPA: Adaptive Hierarchical Prior Alignment for Diffusion Transformers
**作者:** Ruibin Min, Yexin Liu, Aimin Pan
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.03317

**相关性说明:** This paper addresses representation alignment in Diffusion Transformers for general image/video generation, NOT 3D human motion generation. While it uses diffusion-based methods, the application domain is completely different - it's about improving image generation quality through表征对齐, not about extracting 3D motion from 2D video, bridging 2D-3D domain gaps, or SMPL/skeleton-based motion representation. The pain points in the research profile (VLM quality control, temporal motion consistency) are not addressed. This is a technical contribution to diffusion model training methodology, not a Computer Graphics / 3D Human Motion Generation paper.

**摘要:** 本文提出Adaptive Hierarchical Prior Alignment (AHPA)框架，解决Diffusion Transformer训练中表征对齐的粒度适配问题。核心思想是利用VAE编码器的多层次特征作为动态监督信号，通过时间步条件的动态路由器自适应选择和权重不同层次的先验，从而在不同信噪比阶段提供匹配的训练监督。


## 📄 论文 2 - 相关性: 30%
**标题:** Large-Scale High-Quality 3D Gaussian Head Reconstruction from Multi-View Captures
**作者:** Evangelos Ntavelis, Sean Wu, Mohamad Shahbazi
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.04035

**相关性说明:** This paper addresses 3D head reconstruction from multi-view captures (static geometry), while the research profile focuses on 3D human motion generation (temporal animation). The application domains are fundamentally different: motion generation requires temporal consistency and dynamics, while this paper deals with static 3D head geometry. While both involve 3D human imagery, this is more related to digital humans/avatars rather than motion generation. The methods are partially related (multi-modal input fusion) but the core focus and pain points don't align.

**摘要:** HeadsUp是一种可扩展的前馈方法，用于从大规模多相机设置重建高质量3D Gaussian头部。该方法使用编码器-解码器架构，通过UV参数化表示将输入视图转换为锚定在中性头部模板上的3D高斯，能够在超过10,000个受试者的大规模数据集上实现最先进的重建质量。


## 📄 论文 3 - 相关性: 25%
**标题:** MHPR: Multidimensional Human Perception and Reasoning Benchmark for Large Vision-Languate Models
**作者:** Kangkang Wang, Qinting Jiang, Wanping Zhang
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.03485

**相关性说明:** The paper is about benchmarking Vision-Language Models for human-centric scene perception (understanding appearance, clothing, pose, social relations in images). While it mentions "virtual digital humans" as a potential application, this is focused on IMAGE UNDERSTANDING, not 3D MOTION GENERATION. The research profile is specifically about Computer Graphics / 3D Human Motion Generation, which requires generating new motion sequences. This paper addresses VLM evaluation for perceiving humans in static scenes, not generating or extracting 3D human motion from video. Different application domain entirely - perception/understanding vs. motion generation.

**摘要:** 本文提出MHPR，一个全面的人本中心场景感知推理联合评估基准，包含多层次数据设计和自动化标注生成pipeline。基准涵盖个体、多人和人物交互维度，评估细粒度属性和高级语义。实验表明格式化SFT数据和RL数据能显著提升模型性能，使用MHPR训练的7B模型能达到与更大模型接近的效果。


## 📄 论文 4 - 相关性: 25%
**标题:** Rethinking Temporal Consistency in Video Object-Centric Learning: From Prediction to Correspondence
**作者:** Zhiyuan Li, Rongzhen Zhao, Wenyan Yang
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.03650

**相关性说明:** This paper addresses temporal consistency in video object-centric learning (slot-based tracking/segmentation), which is a different application domain from 3D Human Motion Generation in Computer Graphics. While it discusses temporal consistency, the goal is object identity maintenance in video understanding, not generating human motion sequences. The methods (slot-based representations, Hungarian matching) and application (MOVi dataset object tracking) are unrelated to diffusion-based motion generation, SMPL/skeleton representations, or bridging 2D video to 3D motion - which are the core pain points in the research profile. The paper uses similar high-level "temporal consistency" concept but in a completely different domain (video object segmentation vs human motion synthesis).

**摘要:** 本文提出Grounded Correspondence框架，用确定性二分匹配取代视频目标中心学习中的学习动态模块，实现零参数时间建模，在多个数据集上取得有竞争力性能。


## 📄 论文 5 - 相关性: 25%
**标题:** AniMatrix: An Anime Video Generation Model that Thinks in Art, Not Physics
**作者:** Tencent HY Team
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.03652

**相关性说明:** The paper is about 2D anime VIDEO generation, not 3D human MOTION generation. Despite using some similar methods (diffusion models, video generation) and mentioning Seedance, the application domain is fundamentally different: anime video generation (2D visual content) vs 3D human motion generation (skeletal/SMPL-based body movements). The pain points are entirely different - this paper addresses artistic style control in anime, not extracting 3D motion priors from 2D video, bridging 2D-to-3D domain gaps, or VLM-based quality control for motion generation.

**摘要:** AniMatrix是一个针对动漫而非物理真实性的视频生成模型,通过双通道条件机制和三步转换(重新定义正确性、覆盖物理先验、区分艺术与失败)来解决物理偏见模型无法处理动漫风格多样性问题。
