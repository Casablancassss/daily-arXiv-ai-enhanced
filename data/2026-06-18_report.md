**作者:** Logan Mann, Yi Xia, Ajit Saravanan
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.17389

**相关性说明:** This paper studies VLM reliability and hallucination detection in general multi-modal AI systems, which is a different application domain from Computer Graphics / Text-to-Motion. While the research profile mentions "VLM-based quality assessment" as a pain point, this paper is not addressing quality assessment of video/motion generation outputs - it's about VLM reliability for reasoning tasks. The application domain mismatch is significant - this is more aligned with AI safety/interpretability research than computer graphics.

**摘要:** 研究VLM可靠性信号，发现传统注意力-置信度假设不成立，可靠性更多与生成动态相关而非视觉定位

**作者:** Yatai Ji, An-Chieh Cheng, Yang Fu
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.17539

**相关性说明:** The paper focuses on spatial VLM reasoning (depth, distance, scene relationships) for general visual understanding, not Computer Graphics/Text-to-Motion. However, both involve VLMs, which provides a loose methodological connection.

**摘要:** SR-REAL是一个用于空间VLM的双路径空间推理框架，通过强化学习结合语言推理（LOR）和检测后推理（DTR）两种互补路径，提升复杂空间推理能力。


## 📄 论文 3 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** MOCHI: Motion Enhancement of Collaborative Human-object Interactions
**作者:** Jiye Lee, Yonghun Choi, Jungdam Won
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.18243

**相关性说明:** This paper addresses motion enhancement for human-object interactions, but the application domain differs significantly from the research profile. While both deal with human motion, MOCHI focuses on enhancing/refining existing MHOI (multi-human-object interaction) motion data through a diffusion-based noise optimization framework. It does NOT address the core pain points: extracting 3D human motion from 2D video inputs, quality control for video generation model outputs, VLM-based quality assessment, or the 2D video to 3D motion domain gap. The paper uses diffusion methods but for motion data refinement rather than video generation or text-to-motion generation from visual inputs.

**摘要:** MOCHI是一个两阶段框架，用于增强协作多人-物体交互(MHOI)数据中的噪声。该方法首先通过优化生成物理上合理的抓握手势，然后将优化后的抓握扩展到完整的手-物体交互序列，最后使用基于扩散的噪声优化框架结合单人运动先验来细化全身运动。
