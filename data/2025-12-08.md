Motivation: 3-流形的三角剖分表示中，其对偶图的树宽是设计参数化算法的关键稀疏性指标。然而，现有表示可能不便于某些计算任务（如量子不变量计算），因此需要开发能在控制树宽等稀疏参数的同时对三角剖分进行有效变换的算法。

Method: 作者提出两种算法：(1) 线性时间算法将给定三角剖分转化为Heegaard图，并证明该过程保持对偶图的树宽；(2) 准线性时间重三角剖分算法，使所得三角剖分的最大边度数不超过9，同时仅适度增加树宽。二者结合用于构造具有受限交叉性质的Heegaard图。

Result: 成功构建了一个固定参数可解（FPT）框架用于计算Kuperberg的3-流形量子不变量；并实现了从任意三角剖分出发，在准线性时间内生成每个附着曲线至多与9条其他曲线相交的Heegaard图。

Conclusion: 通过控制三角剖分的组合稀疏性（特别是树宽和边度数），可以高效地将其转化为适合特定拓扑计算（如量子不变量）的Heegaard图形式，从而推动了3-流形算法拓扑中的参数化算法设计。

Abstract: 3-manifolds are commonly represented as triangulations, consisting of abstract tetrahedra whose triangular faces are identified in pairs. The combinatorial sparsity of a triangulation, as measured by the treewidth of its dual graph, plays a fundamental role in the design of parameterized algorithms. In this work, we investigate algorithmic procedures that transform or modify a given triangulation while controlling specific sparsity parameters. First, we describe a linear-time algorithm that converts a given triangulation into a Heegaard diagram of the underlying 3-manifold, showing that the construction preserves treewidth. We apply this construction to exhibit a fixed-parameter tractable framework for computing Kuperberg's quantum invariants of 3-manifolds. Second, we present a quasi-linear-time algorithm that retriangulates a given triangulation into one with maximum edge valence of at most nine, while only moderately increasing the treewidth of the dual graph. Combining these two algorithms yields a quasi-linear-time algorithm that produces, from a given triangulation, a Heegaard diagram in which every attaching curve intersects at most nine others.
