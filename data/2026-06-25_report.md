## 📄 论文 1 - 相关性: 55%
**标题:** Social Structure Matters in 3D Human-Human Interaction Generation
**作者:** Zhongju Wang, Beier Wang, Yatao Bian
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.24255

**相关性说明:** This paper addresses 3D human motion generation (text-to-motion) which matches the general application domain. However, it focuses on human-human interaction (dyadic motion) rather than single-person motion generation, and uses LLM-based planning rather than the visual-guided approaches, VLM screening, or diffusion-based motion synthesis methods specified in the research profile. The pain points about 2D-to-3D motion extraction and visual-guided feature extraction are not addressed. The method (LLM + LoRA-adapted motion model) differs from the profile's visual/VLM/diffusion methods.

**摘要:** 本文提出Solo-to-Social框架,将文本驱动的3D人际交互生成问题分解为社交结构建模和动作实现两个阶段,利用LLM作为规划器进行社交结构推理,结合适配的独行动作模型作为执行器生成协调的双人动作。


## 📄 论文 2 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** Tri-Efficient Transfer Learning for Point Cloud Videos
**作者:** Yiding Sun, Dongxu Zhang, Jihua Zhu
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.24175

**相关性说明:** This paper addresses point cloud video understanding (action recognition and semantic segmentation), which is a different application domain from text-to-motion in 3D human motion generation. While both deal with motion data, the focus is on recognizing/classifying motions from point clouds rather than generating human motions from text or video. The paper uses some related techniques (LoRA, contrastive learning) but targets point cloud scene understanding rather than the cross-modal alignment between 2D video and 3D motion space that the research profile requires. It's a moderate methodological match but wrong application domain.

**摘要:** 本文提出PoinTriE框架,针对点云视频理解中的三个核心挑战:数据效率、参数效率和内存效率。该方法通过合成伪运动轨迹、文本语料库和2D投影来生成自监督信号,采用几何-运动对偶网络进行多模态对比学习,在微调阶段使用带有LoRA单元的轻量级时空侧网络,并通过梯度流掩码策略显著降低内存消耗。实验表明该方法在动作识别和语义分割任务上达到了最先进水平。


## 📄 论文 3 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** SignNet-1M: Large-Scale Multilingual Sign Language Video Dataset with Downstream Benchmarks
**作者:** Zhewen He, Junyi Hu, Haomian Huang
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.24361

**相关性说明:** This paper focuses on sign language video dataset generation (ASL/CSL/German), which is primarily a computer vision/speech processing task rather than Computer Graphics Text-to-Motion generation. While it uses 3D Gaussian splatting and diffusion models, the application domain is different - it's about creating training data for sign language recognition, not generating 3D human motion from text. The pain points don't align: there's no mention of 2D-to-3D motion extraction, VLM-based quality assessment, or cross-modal alignment between 2D video and 3D motion. Some methods overlap (diffusion, 3D representation), but the core application domain mismatch makes this only marginally relevant at best.

**摘要:** SignNet-1M是一个大规模手语数据集，通过3D高斯溅射、扩散模型和后处理增强技术，生成包含ASL、CSL和德语手语的多样化训练数据，显著提升模型在跨视角、跨背景、跨身份等真实场景下的鲁棒性。


## 📄 论文 4 - 相关性: 30%
**标题:** SER: Learning to Ground Video Reasoning with Semantic Evidence Rewards
**作者:** Sheng Xia, Zhengqin Lai, Tianxiang Jiang
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.24726

**相关性说明:** This paper addresses video reasoning and VQA tasks using VLMs, which is a different application domain from the research profile's focus on Text-to-Motion in 3D Human Motion Generation. While both involve VLMs and video, the paper aims to improve temporal reasoning in video QA (answering questions about video content), not generating or extracting 3D human motion from video. The pain points in the profile are about cross-modal alignment between 2D video and 3D motion space, and visual-guided feature extraction for motion generation models - none of which are addressed by this paper's video reasoning approach.

**摘要:** 本文提出语义证据奖励(SER)方法,通过引入 referee VLM 作为局部检查器,评估模型生成的证据声明的相关性和定位质量,结合时间惩罚来增强视频多模态大语言模型的时空推理能力。


## 📄 论文 5 - 相关性: 28%
**标题:** EgoSAT: A Comprehensive Benchmark of Egocentric Streaming Interaction Understanding
**作者:** Yijia Lei, Jinzhao Li, Yichi Zhang
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.24422

**相关性说明:** This paper is about egocentric video understanding and reasoning (analyzing/understanding what happens in first-person videos), not about 3D human motion generation. While both mention VLMs, the application domains are fundamentally different: the research profile focuses on generating 3D human motions from text/video (text-to-motion synthesis), while EgoSAT focuses on evaluating VLMs' reasoning capabilities about video content (understanding, prediction). The pain points in the profile are about motion extraction, quality assessment for motion generation, and cross-modal alignment for generating motion - none of which this paper addresses. The paper evaluates VLMs on video QA tasks, not on extracting 3D motion information or generating motions.

**摘要:** EgoSAT是首个针对流式设置下自我中心视频推理的综合基准，用于评估现代视觉语言模型(VLMs)的能力。该基准包含1,997个独特视频（约165小时自我中心 footage）和约4,800个高质量问答对，统一了回顾性推理、在线理解和前瞻性预测三种任务。
