## 📄 论文 1 - 相关性: 55%
**标题:** TrajectoryMover: Generative Movement of Object Trajectories in Videos
**作者:** Kiran Chhatre, Hyeonho Jeong, Yulia Gryaditskaya
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.29092

**相关性说明:** This paper is about video generation with trajectory movement in the computer graphics domain. It addresses the pain point of bridging 2D video and 3D motion by maintaining relative 3D motion when moving object trajectories. However, it doesn't directly address the other pain points (extracting visual guidance features, quality control for Wanxiang/Seedance, VLM accuracy). The method (TrajectoryMover video generator) is in the same general area as the mentioned video generation models but doesn't use VLM or cross-modal knowledge distillation. Overall moderately relevant as it's in the same application domain but doesn't address the specific methods or quality control concerns in the profile.

**摘要:** 本文提出了TrajectoryAtlas数据生成管道和TrajectoryMover视频生成器，能够在保持相对3D运动的同时移动对象的3D运动轨迹，解决了现有生成式视频编辑方法无法移动对象3D轨迹的问题。


## 📄 论文 2 - 相关性: 55%
**标题:** Self-Consistency for LLM-Based Motion Trajectory Generation and Verification
**作者:** Jiaju Ma, R. Kenny Jones, Jiajun Wu
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.29301

**相关性说明:** This paper focuses on LLM-based motion trajectory generation and verification using geometric transformations, which is a different application domain from the research profile's focus on video generation models (Wanxiang/Seedance) and VLMs for video quality control. While both relate to computer graphics and motion/trajectory generation, the methods differ significantly - the profile emphasizes video generation and VLM-based quality supervision, whereas this paper uses LLMs with geometric transformation clustering. The paper does address quality verification but for motion trajectories rather than video generation quality control, making it moderately but not highly relevant.

**摘要:** 将自洽性（self-consistency）技术从自然语言领域扩展到视觉领域，用于改进LLM生成的运动图形轨迹质量。通过对LLM采样的轨迹进行聚类，并利用几何变换（刚性、相似性、仿射）建模形状家族来判断轨迹一致性。


## 📄 论文 3 - 相关性: 50%
**标题:** GRVS: a Generalizable and Recurrent Approach to Monocular Dynamic View Synthesis
**作者:** Thomas Tanay, Mohammed Brahimi, Michal Nazarczuk
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.29734

**相关性说明:** The paper is about dynamic view synthesis (novel view synthesis) - a computer graphics topic - but it doesn't address the research profile's pain points. The profile focuses on video generation quality control (Wanxiang/Seedance), VLM-based supervision, and bridging 2D video to 3D motion. GRVS is about rendering new camera views from monocular video, not about video generation models, quality supervision, or feature extraction for motion generation. The application domain is technically computer graphics but addresses a fundamentally different problem than what the profile seeks.

**摘要:** 针对动态场景单目视频新视角合成问题，提出一种可泛化模型，包含递归循环和动态平面扫描两个组件，能够分离相机和场景运动，实现六自由度控制，在几何细节重建上 outperforms 现有方法。


## 📄 论文 4 - 相关性: 50%
**标题:** Gloria: Consistent Character Video Generation via Content Anchors
**作者:** Yuhang Yang, Fan Zhang, Huaijin Pi
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.29931

**相关性说明:** The paper addresses character video generation (video generation in Computer Graphics), which overlaps with part of the research profile's domain. However, it does not address the specific pain points: (1) Extracting visual guidance features from 2D video output is not addressed - the paper focuses on generating videos, not extracting features from them; (2) Quality control via VLM is not mentioned; (3) Bridging 2D video to 3D motion is not addressed; (4) Cross-modal knowledge distillation is not part of the method. While both are in Computer Graphics and involve video generation, the paper uses different methods (anchor frames, Superset Content Anchoring, RoPE) rather than the specified combination of VLM, 3D motion generation models, and feature extraction for quality supervision.

**摘要:** 一种基于锚帧的角色视频生成方法，通过Superset Content Anchoring和RoPE as Weak Condition机制解决长时一致性难题，可生成长达10分钟的角色视频。


## 📄 论文 5 - 相关性: 45%

**作者:** Ryosuke Matsuda, Keito Kudo, Haruto Yoshida
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.29186

**相关性说明:** This paper is about evaluating text-to-long video generation systems, not about video generation or feature extraction. While both are in the computer graphics/video domain, the paper focuses on creating an evaluation benchmark (quality assessment), whereas the research profile focuses on video generation models (Wanxiang/Seedance), extracting visual guidance features from 2D video, and bridging 2D video to 3D motion. The paper doesn't address the core pain points of extracting visual guidance features or bridging 2D-to-3D domain gap. It partially addresses quality control for video generation but from an evaluation perspective, not from a generation or feature extraction perspective.

**摘要:** 本文提出SLVMEval基准，用于元评估文本到视频(T2V)评估系统在长视频（最长约3小时）上的表现，该基准通过成对比较框架和人群ourcing筛选，创建了10个不同方面的可控测试集。
