- 方法: Video Generation Models (Wan Xiang, Seedance), Vision Language Models (VLM), 3D Motion Generation Models, Visual Feature Extraction, Quality Assessment and Filtering
- 痛点: Extracting reliable 3D visual guidance features from 2D video outputs, Ensuring VLM-based quality assessment aligns with motion fidelity, Bridging the domain gap between 2D video prior and 3D motion generation

## 📄 论文 1 - 相关性: 45%
**标题:** VLMs Need Words: Vision Language Models Ignore Visual Detail In Favor of Semantic Anchors
**作者:** Haz Sameen Shahgir, Xiaofu Chen, Yu Fu
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.02486

**相关性说明:** The paper studies VLMs limitations in fine-grained visual perception (visual correspondence tasks), which tangentially relates to leveraging VLMs for quality assessment in text-to-motion pipelines - one of the pain points. However, the application domain is fundamentally different: this paper focuses on general VLM behavior and semantic understanding, not 3D motion generation, video-to-motion synthesis, or computer graphics. The paper does not address extracting 3D guidance features from 2D video or bridging 2D video-to-3D motion domain gaps. While VLMs are mentioned in the research profile methods, this paper doesn't target the motion generation domain at all.

**摘要:** VLMs在需要细粒度视觉感知的任务上表现不佳，即使所需信息存在于内部表示中。这是因为训练管道侧重于将视觉信息映射到文本空间，导致视觉-focused任务如视觉对应和新型视觉实体推理支持不足。


## 📄 论文 2 - 相关性: 45%
**标题:** UNICA: A Unified Neural Framework for Controllable 3D Avatars
**作者:** Jiahe Zhu, Xinyao Wang, Yiyu Zhuang
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.02799

**相关性说明:** The paper is about 3D avatar generation from action/keyboard inputs, not Text-to-Motion. It uses action-conditioned diffusion on 2D position maps (to 3D avatar geometry), whereas the profile needs text-to-motion generation. No mention of VLM-based quality assessment, 2D video to 3D motion extraction, or bridging 2D video priors to 3D motion. The application domain is different - it's avatar animation control rather than text-driven human motion generation.

**摘要:** UNICA是一个无骨架的生成模型，通过统一的神经框架整合了3D avatar的所有控制组件。该方法使用动作条件扩散模型在2D位置图上生成几何体，并通过点Transformer映射到3D高斯溅射进行高保真渲染，自然捕捉头发和服装动态，支持超长自回归生成。


## 📄 论文 3 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** VERTIGO: Visual Preference Optimization for Cinematic Camera Trajectory Generation
**作者:** Mengtian Li, Yuwei Lu, Feifei Li
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.02467

**相关性说明:** The paper addresses CAMERA trajectory generation, not HUMAN motion generation. While both involve motion in 3D space and use VLMs for quality assessment, the application domains are fundamentally different. VERTIGO generates how a camera moves through a scene, while the research profile needs text-to-human-motion generation. The methods (VLM-based assessment, 2D-3D bridging) are somewhat similar, but the target motion is completely different - camera motion vs. human body motion. This is similar to using diffusion for image generation vs. human motion generation - same method category but different application domains.

**摘要:** VERTIGO是首个用于相机轨迹生成器的视觉偏好优化框架，通过Unity引擎渲染2D视觉预览，使用微调的视觉语言模型结合循环语义相似性机制进行评分，并通过直接偏好优化(DPO)进行后训练。


## 📄 论文 4 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** THOM: Generating Physically Plausible Hand-Object Meshes From Text
**作者:** Uyoung Jeong, Yihalem Yimolal Tiruneh, Hyung Jin Chang
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.02736

**相关性说明:** Key mismatch: The research profile is about Text-to-Motion (generating temporal motion sequences), while THOM is about Text-to-3D Mesh generation (static hand-object meshes). While both deal with text-to-3D generation, "motion" in computer graphics refers to temporal dynamics/animation, not static 3D geometry. The paper doesn't address the pain points about 2D video-to-3D motion extraction or bridging 2D video priors. It uses VLM but for mesh quality assessment, not motion fidelity. The application domain is fundamentally different.

**摘要:** THOM是一个无需训练的框架,能从文本生成具有逼真视觉质量和物理合理性的3D手-物体交互(HOI)网格。


## 📄 论文 5 - 相关性: 30%
**标题:** Not All Frames Deserve Full Computation: Accelerating Autoregressive Video Generation via Selective Computation and Predictive Extrapolation
**作者:** Hanshuai Cui, Zhiqing Tang, Zhi Yao
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.02979

**相关性说明:** This paper focuses on accelerating autoregressive video generation (MAGI-1, SkyReels-V2 are video generation models), which is a different application domain from Text-to-Motion. While both may use diffusion models, the paper addresses computational efficiency for 2D video generation, not 3D human motion generation from text. The pain points in the research profile involve extracting 3D guidance from video and bridging 2D-to-3D for motion generation, whereas this paper is about video generation acceleration. This is a case of using similar methods in a different application domain.

**摘要:** SCOPE是一种用于高效自回归视频扩散的训练-free框架，通过三模态调度器（缓存、预测、重计算）和选择性计算策略解决现有二元决策和均匀处理的问题，在MAGI-1和SkyReels-V2上实现最高4.73倍加速。
