# 📊 每日 arXiv 论文推荐

**研究画像:**
- 领域: Computer Graphics / 3D Motion Generation
- 方法: Video Generation Models (Wanxiang/Seedance), Vision Language Model (VLM), 3D Motion Generation Model, Visual Guidance Feature Extraction, Cross-modal Learning
- 痛点: Reliable visual feature extraction from 2D video to 3D motion, Quality control and filtering of generated motion via VLM, Effective integration of 2D video priors into 3D motion models, Temporal consistency in motion generation

**最相关的 5 篇论文:**


## 📄 论文 1 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** SLARM: Streaming and Language-Aligned Reconstruction Model for Dynamic Scenes
**作者:** Zhicheng Qiu, Jiarui Meng, Tong-an Luo
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.22893

**相关性说明:** SLARM is about dynamic scene RECONSTRUCTION (reconstructing 3D scenes from video), not 3D MOTION GENERATION. These are fundamentally different application domains. While SLARM does extract visual features from 2D video and uses differentiable rendering, its goal is reconstructing existing scenes rather than generating new motion. The research profile specifically needs motion generation models (like Seedance/Wanxiang), VLM for quality control, and cross-modal learning for video-to-motion conversion. SLARM focuses on semantic understanding and streaming inference for scene reconstruction, which doesn't address the pain points of 2D video-to-3D motion generation, VLM-based quality filtering, or effective integration of video priors into motion models.

**摘要:** SLARM是一种统一的前馈模型,用于动态场景重建、语义理解和实时流式推理,无需任何光流监督,仅通过可微分渲染进行训练。


## 📄 论文 2 - 相关性: 30%
**标题:** SIMART: Decomposing Monolithic Meshes into Sim-ready Articulated Assets via MLLM
**作者:** Chuanrui Zhang, Minghan Qin, Yuang Wang
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.23386

**相关性说明:** This paper focuses on decomposing static 3D meshes into articulated assets for robotics simulation - a different application domain than the research profile's focus on 3D motion generation from 2D video. The paper addresses static mesh decomposition and kinematics prediction, not temporal motion generation from video. It doesn't address any of the stated pain points (2D-to-3D extraction, VLM quality control, video priors integration, temporal consistency). While both are in computer graphics, the core task and methods are fundamentally different.

**摘要:** 提出SIMART统一MLLM框架，通过Sparse 3D VQ-VAE将3D标记减少70%，实现部件级分解和运动学预测的联合学习，在关节3D资产生成上达到SOTA性能。


## 📄 论文 3 - 相关性: 25%
**标题:** TrajLoom: Dense Future Trajectory Generation from Video
**作者:** Zewei Zhang, Jia Jun Cheng Xian, Kaiwen Liu
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.22606

**相关性说明:** The paper focuses on 2D trajectory prediction from video (dense point trajectories in pixel space), while the research profile is about 3D Motion Generation in Computer Graphics. This is a fundamental domain mismatch - the paper deals with 2D trajectories that stay in the image plane, not 3D human motion or 3D scene motion generation. The methods (VAE, flow matching for trajectory prediction) and application (video understanding, video generation support) are different from the 3D motion generation domain. While temporal consistency is somewhat relevant, the core application domain and the fact that this stays in 2D pixel space rather than going to 3D makes it largely irrelevant for someone working on 3D motion generation.

**摘要:** 提出TrajLoom框架预测视频中密集点轨迹的未来演化，通过Grid-Anchor Offset Encoding减少位置偏差，TrajLoom-VAE学习时空潜在表示，TrajLoom-Flow进行流匹配生成，将预测范围从24帧扩展到81帧，并引入统一基准TrajLoomBench。


## 📄 论文 4 - 相关性: 25%
**标题:** Reconstruction-Guided Slot Curriculum: Addressing Object Over-Fragmentation in Video Object-Centric Learning
**作者:** WonJun Moon, Hyun Seok Seong, Jae-Pil Heo
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.22758

**相关性说明:** This paper addresses video object-centric learning with slot attention for object segmentation/disentanglement, which is a fundamentally different application domain from 3D Motion Generation. While both deal with video data and the paper mentions temporal consistency, the goal is unsupervised object discovery in videos (segmenting objects), NOT extracting features for or generating 3D motion. The methods (slot attention, curriculum learning) are used for scene decomposition, not for motion generation or cross-modal learning between video and motion domains. The paper's "temporal consistency" is about object consistency across frames, not motion temporal smoothness. This is a computer vision/video understanding paper, not a computer graphics/motion generation paper.

**摘要:** SlotCurri是一种重建引导的视频目标中心学习槽位课程方法，通过逐步分配槽位解决过度分割问题，并结合结构感知损失和循环推理提升性能。


## 📄 论文 5 - 相关性: 25%
**标题:** PhotoAgent: A Robotic Photographer with Spatial and Aesthetic Understanding
**作者:** Lirong Che, Zhenfeng Gan, Yanbo Chen
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.22796

**相关性说明:** This paper is about robotic photography and viewpoint optimization using 3DGS world models, NOT about 3D motion generation. The application domain is fundamentally different - it's focused on controlling a robot photographer to find optimal viewing angles for creative photography, not generating 3D motion from video. While both involve 3D understanding and VLM/LMM, the goals are completely different: one optimizes camera viewpoints, the other generates human motion. The pain points and methods (despite some superficial overlap with VLM) are not aligned with the research profile's needs for motion generation from video.

**摘要:** PhotoAgent是一个结合大模型推理与3DGS世界模型的具身智能体,用于执行创意摄影任务,通过将主观美学目标转化为几何约束并在内视界模型中迭代优化视角来实现高质量成像。
