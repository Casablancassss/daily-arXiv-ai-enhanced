## 📄 论文 1 - 相关性: 50%
**标题:** MolmoMotion: Forecasting Point Trajectories in 3D with Language Instruction
**作者:** Jianing Zhang, Chenhao Zheng, Yajun Yang
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.18558

**相关性说明:** This paper addresses 3D motion prediction which is somewhat related to the research profile's interest in 3D motion generation. However, the application domain is different: MolmoMotion focuses on physical motion forecasting for robot manipulation and physical reasoning (predicting point trajectories), not computer graphics animation synthesis. The method uses autoregressive prediction and flow matching rather than the diffusion-based video generation models (Wanxiang, Seedance) mentioned in the profile. While it deals with 3D trajectories and temporal modeling, it doesn't address the specific pain points about video-generated motion priors, quality supervision mechanisms, or semantic gap between 2D visual features and 3D motion representation in the context of computer graphics.

**摘要:** 本文提出了MolmoMotion，一个用于3D点运动预测的通用模型，通过大规模数据集MolmoMotion-1M（包含116万段视频注释的3D点轨迹）和基准测试集PointMotionBench（涵盖111个物体类别和61种运动类型）进行训练和评估。该方法支持自回归坐标预测和流匹配轨迹生成两种模式，在多样化的运动模式预测上表现出色，并能迁移到机器人操作和视频生成等下游任务。


## 📄 论文 2 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** UniTemp: Unlocking Video Generation in Any Temporal Order via Bidirectional Distillation
**作者:** Lin Zhang, Sicheng Mo, Zefan Cai
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.18702

**相关性说明:** This paper is about general video generation using diffusion models, not about 3D motion generation from video. The research profile specifically targets video-generated motion priors for 3D character animation with skeleton-based motion representation, while UniTemp focuses on bidirectional video content creation (short films, visual stories). Although both use diffusion models, they address completely different application domains - general video generation vs. skeleton-based 3D motion extraction. The paper doesn't address any of the pain points related to 2D-to-3D motion semantic gap, visual guidance feature extraction, or quality supervision for motion realism.

**摘要:** 本文提出UniTemp框架，解决自回归视频扩散模型只能单向(从前到后)生成的问题，实现任意时间方向的视频生成。


## 📄 论文 3 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** Taming I2V models for Image HOI Editing: A Cognitive Benchmark and Agentic Self-Correcting Framework
**作者:** Jiayi Gao, Qingchao Chen, Yuxin Peng
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.19073

**相关性说明:** While both use diffusion-based methods, this paper focuses on 2D Image-to-Video (I2V) editing for Human-Object Interaction, not 3D motion generation/synthesis. The research profile specifically needs skeleton-based 3D motion generation, temporal animation synthesis, and semantic mapping from 2D video to 3D motion representation. The paper addresses 2D image editing rather than the core computer graphics problems of extracting visual guidance features for 3D animation or maintaining temporal consistency in synthesized 3D animations. This is a different application domain entirely - using similar diffusion methods but for image editing rather than computer graphics motion synthesis.

**摘要:** 本文提出HOI-Edit基准和SCPE框架，解决现有图像编辑方法无法处理复杂人物-物体交互(HOI)的问题，通过图像到视频模型的迭代提示约束实现自纠正编辑。


## 📄 论文 4 - 相关性: 30%
**标题:** Data-Forcing Distillation: Restoring Diversity and Fidelity in Few-Step Video Generation
**作者:** Siyi Chen, Shaowei Liu, Yixuan Jia
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.18478

**相关性说明:** This paper addresses 2D VIDEO GENERATION (creating video content), while the research profile is about Computer Graphics focused on 3D MOTION GENERATION from video, skeleton-based motion representation, and semantic gap between 2D visual features and 3D motion. These are fundamentally different application domains. Although both use diffusion-based video generation models (Wanxiang mentioned), the paper's focus on model distillation to fix diversity/saturation issues in 2D video generation does not address the pain points of extracting visual guidance features for 3D motion synthesis or maintaining temporal consistency in 3D animations.

**摘要:** 本文提出Data-Forcing Distillation (DFD)框架，通过教师得分差异指导学生模型恢复真实数据分布，解决DMD方法中样本多样性下降和过度饱和问题，仅需100-300步微调即可在视频生成模型上取得优于教师模型的效果。


## 📄 论文 5 - 相关性: 30%
**标题:** Motion-Focused Latent Action Enables Cross-Embodiment VLA Training from Human EgoVideos
**作者:** Runze Xu, Yiluo Zhang, Jian Wang
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.18955

**相关性说明:** Wrong application domain. The paper is about ROBOTICS/VLA training (robot manipulation) not Computer Graphics/3D animation synthesis. While it uses VLM and video analysis, the goal is robotic control from human ego-videos, not video-generated motion priors or 3D animation synthesis. Robot motion generation is fundamentally different from human motion generation in computer graphics. The pain points are also different - this paper addresses robot learning rather than motion fidelity, temporal consistency, or semantic gap in 3D animation synthesis.

**摘要:** 提出了一种基于潜动作的框架，从无标注的人类操作视频中提取通用动作先验知识，通过 Hybrid Disentangled VQ-VAE 解耦运动动力学与环境背景，构建跨化身动作码书，实现仅需50条轨迹即可完成下游适应的竞争性性能。
