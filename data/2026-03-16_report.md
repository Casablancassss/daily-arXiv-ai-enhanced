## 📄 论文 1 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** Geometry-Guided Camera Motion Understanding in VideoLLMs
**作者:** Haoan Feng, Sri Harsha Musunuri, Guan-Ming Su
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.13119

**相关性说明:** The paper focuses on camera motion UNDERSTANDING and recognition in VideoLLMs (diagnosing VideoLLMs' ability to recognize camera motion), while the research profile is about 3D MOTION GENERATION from 2D video. These are fundamentally different tasks: recognition vs. generation. The paper doesn't address extracting 3D motion priors for generation, quality control in motion generation, or cross-modal alignment for creating motion outputs. Although both deal with video/motion in computer vision, the application domain is different - this paper is about motion analysis/recognition, not motion generation.

**摘要:** 针对VideoLLM相机运动识别能力不足的问题，构建了数据集和基准进行诊断，发现视觉编码器对相机运动线索表示薄弱，提出利用3D基础模型提取几何线索并通过结构化提示注入的轻量级解决方案。


## 📄 论文 2 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** InterEdit: Navigating Text-Guided Multi-Human 3D Motion Editing
**作者:** Yebin Yang, Di Wen, Lei Qi
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.13082

**相关性说明:** This paper addresses text-guided 3D motion EDITING, while the research profile focuses on extracting 3D motion from 2D VIDEO content. The application domains are different: the profile is about video-to-motion generation, while this paper is about editing existing motion sequences using text. While both involve 3D human motion and use diffusion models, the core task differs - motion editing vs. video-based motion extraction. The paper doesn't address the pain points about 2D video priors, VLM quality control, or visual-guided feature extraction from video.

**摘要:** 本文提出InterEdit3D数据集和TMME基准，用于研究文本引导的多人3D运动编辑任务。提出InterEdit模型，采用同步无分类器条件扩散架构，通过语义感知计划令牌对齐和交互感知频率令牌对齐技术，有效捕捉高层交互线索和周期性运动动态，在多人运动编辑任务中取得了最先进的性能。


## 📄 论文 3 - 相关性: 30%
**标题:** SAW: Toward a Surgical Action World Model via Controllable and Scalable Video Generation
**作者:** Sampath Rapuri, Lalithkumar Seenivasan, Dominik Schneider
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.13024

**相关性说明:** While this paper is in Computer Vision and uses video diffusion methods, the APPLICATION DOMAIN is completely different. The research profile focuses on 3D motion generation (extracting 3D motion priors from 2D video, bridging 2D video to 3D motion output), whereas this paper focuses on SURGICAL VIDEO GENERATION - a medical domain application. The paper generates 2D surgical videos from trajectories, not 3D motion. It does not address VLM for quality supervision, does not produce 3D motion output, and the surgical domain is fundamentally different from human motion generation in computer graphics. This is a case of using similar methods (video diffusion) but in a completely different application domain (medical surgery vs. human motion graphics).

**摘要:** SAW是一个手术动作世界模型，通过视频扩散技术生成逼真的外科手术 action 视频，仅需四个轻量级条件信号：语言提示、参考手术场景、组织 affordance 掩码和2D工具尖端轨迹。


## 📄 论文 4 - 相关性: 25%
**标题:** SPARROW: Learning Spatial Precision and Temporal Referential Consistency in Pixel-Grounded Video MLLMs
**作者:** Mohamad Alansari, Naufal Suryanto, Divya Velayudhan
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.12382

**相关性说明:** This paper (SPARROW) focuses on video visual grounding and understanding - specifically addressing spatial precision and temporal consistency in video MLLMs for tasks like RVOS, visual grounding, and referring expression comprehension. While it uses VLMs, this is for 2D video understanding tasks. 

The research profile explicitly focuses on: (1) Extracting 3D motion priors from 2D video content, (2) 3D Motion Generation Models, (3) Bridging 2D video priors to accurate 3D motion output - which is fundamentally different from video understanding/grounding. The application domain is 3D motion generation vs 2D video grounding - these are completely different areas within Computer Vision, even though both deal with video. Methods like video generation models (Wanxiang/万相, Seedance) and 3D Motion Generation are not addressed in this paper.

**摘要:** SPARROW是一个像素级视频MLLM，通过目标特定跟踪特征和双提示设计解决视频中空间精度与时间一致性的难题，在三个开源模型和六个基准上取得显著性能提升。


## 📄 论文 5 - 相关性: 25%
**标题:** Do You See What I Am Pointing At? Gesture-Based Egocentric Video Question Answering
**作者:** Yura Choi, Roy Miles, Rolandos Alexandros Potamias
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.12533

**相关性说明:** This paper focuses on gesture-based egocentric video question answering for AI assistants using 3D hand keypoints. While it does use 3D hand keypoint extraction from video (similar method), the APPLICATION DOMAIN is completely different - this is about video understanding/VQA for human-computer interaction, NOT about 3D motion generation for computer graphics. The research profile specifically targets extracting 3D motion priors for motion generation models (like human motion synthesis), not gesture recognition for question answering. The pain points are also different: profile focuses on 3D motion generation quality, cross-modal alignment for motion, while paper focuses on understanding pointing gestures in egocentric video.

**摘要:** 针对自我中心AI助手的指向手势问答问题，提出EgoPointVQA数据集和HINT模型，通过3D手部关键点编码提供空间时间上下文，HINT-14B准确率68.1%，超越InternVL3-14B达6.6%。
