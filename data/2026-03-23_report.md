## 📄 论文 1 - 相关性: 85%
**标题:** MuSteerNet: Human Reaction Generation from Videos via Observation-Reaction Mutual Steering
**作者:** Yuan Zhou, Yongzhi Li, Yanqi Dai
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.20187

**相关性说明:** Direct domain match - this paper is specifically about 3D human motion generation from video (video-to-motion), which exactly matches the research profile. It addresses key pain points: bridging the 2D video to 3D motion domain gap, extracting reliable visual features from video priors, and improving quality/reliability of generated motions. The method uses a two-stage framework with visual feature refinement (Prototype Feedback Steering) and reaction refinement - similar to the video-to-motion pipeline in the profile. The only minor gap is it doesn't specifically mention VLM for quality supervision or text alignment, but the core application domain and methodology are highly aligned.

**摘要:** MuSteerNet是一个通过观察-反应相互引导的视频驱动人体反应生成框架，旨在解决现有方法中视觉观察与反应类型之间严重关系失真问题，从而生成与视频内容匹配的人体3D动作。


## 📄 论文 2 - 相关性: 30%
**标题:** Physion-Eval: Evaluating Physical Realism in Generated Video via Human Reasoning
**作者:** Qin Zhang, Peiyu Jing, Hong-Xing Yu
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.19607

**相关性说明:** This paper focuses on evaluating physical realism in general AI-generated videos (world simulation), not 3D human motion generation. While it mentions video generation models like 万相 and Seedance, the application domain is fundamentally different - it's about video quality assessment rather than generating 3D human motions from 2D visual priors. The paper addresses physical realism in videos but doesn't bridge 2D video frames to 3D motion space or address motion generation quality control specifically. It's a tangential related work at best, focusing on evaluation methodology for video generation rather than the core 3D human motion generation pipeline.

**摘要:** Physion-Eval是一个用于诊断AI生成视频物理真实感失败的大规模基准测试，包含10,990条专家推理轨迹，涵盖22个细粒度物理类别，发现83.3%的外视角和93.5%的内视角生成视频存在至少一个人类可识别的物理故障。


## 📄 论文 3 - 相关性: 25%
**标题:** RAM: Recover Any 3D Human Motion in-the-Wild
**作者:** Sen Jia, Ning Zhu, Jinqin Zhong
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.19929

**相关性说明:** This paper is about 3D human motion CAPTURE (recovering/estimating motion from video), not 3D human motion GENERATION. The research profile specifically needs methods for GENERATING new 3D motions (like video-to-motion generation frameworks). Motion capture/recovery reconstructs existing motions from video, while motion generation creates new, possibly never-before-seen motions. The paper addresses pose estimation and tracking challenges, not motion generation quality control, visual-text alignment, or two-stage generation frameworks mentioned in the profile.

**摘要:** RAM是一个用于野外无标记3D人体运动捕获的多人跟踪框架，通过运动感知语义跟踪器、记忆增强的时序HMR模块、轻量级预测器和门控组合器实现鲁棒的身份关联和连贯的运动估计。


## 📄 论文 4 - 相关性: 25%
**标题:** OrbitNVS: Harnessing Video Diffusion Priors for Novel View Synthesis
**作者:** Jinglin Liang, Zijian Zhou, Rui Huang
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.19613

**相关性说明:** This paper is about Novel View Synthesis (NVS) - generating different camera views of objects/scenes - which is a completely different application domain from 3D Human Motion Generation. While both use video diffusion models, the core application is fundamentally different: NVS focuses on view synthesis for 3D reconstruction, not human skeletal motion generation. The pain points are also misaligned - this paper addresses geometric consistency in view synthesis rather than bridging 2D video-to-3D motion space or ensuring visual-text alignment in human motion generation. Using diffusion models for object view synthesis is not comparable to using diffusion models for human motion generation.

**摘要:** OrbitNVS将新颖视角合成(NVS)重新定义为轨道视频生成任务，通过adapt预训练视频生成模型来实现高质量视角合成。该方法引入相机适配器实现精确相机控制，设计法线图生成分支增强几何一致性，并使用像素空间监督缓解潜在空间压缩导致的模糊外观。在GSO和OmniObject3D基准数据集上显著优于先前方法，尤其在单视角设置下PSNR提升2.9dB和2.4dB。
