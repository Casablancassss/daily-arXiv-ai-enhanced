## 📄 论文 1 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** Unified Multimodal Uncertain Inference
**作者:** Dengjia Zhang, Alexander Martin, William Jurayj
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.08701

**相关性说明:** The paper focuses on uncertainty estimation and calibrated probability reasoning across text, audio, and video modalities. While it uses multi-modal learning approaches, this is a general AI/ML method paper about uncertainty quantification, not specifically about Computer Graphics/Computer Vision or 2D-to-3D motion generation. The application domain is fundamentally different - this is about general multi-modal reasoning, not 3D motion synthesis from video. The pain points in the profile (visual guidance feature extraction, motion realism, temporal consistency, text-to-3D alignment) are not addressed by this paper on uncertainty estimation.

**摘要:** UMUI是一个新的多模态推理任务，跨越文本、音频和视频，要求模型产生校准的概率估计。CLUE方法通过自一致性教师校准和分布置信度探测，使3B模型超越32B基线性能。


## 📄 论文 2 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** BIAS: A Biologically Inspired Algorithm for Video Saliency Detection
**作者:** Zhao-ji Zhang, Ya-tang Li
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.08858

**相关性说明:** This paper focuses on video saliency detection for visual attention modeling and accident prediction, which is a different application domain from 2D video to 3D motion conversion. While both are in Computer Vision, the paper addresses saliency detection rather than visual guidance feature extraction for 3D motion generation. The methods (biologically inspired algorithms, retina-inspired motion detection) are also different from the research profile's focus on video generation models, VLMs, and 3D motion generation models. The paper doesn't address the pain points of motion realism, temporal consistency for 3D conversion, or text-to-motion alignment.

**摘要:** BIAS是一种结合生物启发的快速动态视觉显著目标检测模型，通过视网膜-inspired运动检测器和多高斯峰拟合算法实现毫秒级延迟，在视频显著目标检测和交通事故预测中取得先进性能。


## 📄 论文 3 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** TAIHRI: Task-Aware 3D Human Keypoints Localization for Close-Range Human-Robot Interaction
**作者:** Ao Li, Yonggen Ling, Yiyang Lin
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.08921

**相关性说明:** This paper focuses on 3D keypoint estimation for human-robot interaction (perceiving human poses for robot manipulation), while the research profile concerns 2D video to 3D motion conversion (generating full-body motion sequences for graphics/animation). The application domains are fundamentally different: TAIHRI solves pose perception for HRI, not motion generation/conversion. While both use VLMs, TAIHRI doesn't address motion realism, temporal consistency, or text-to-motion alignment - it estimates sparse 3D keypoints in egocentric view for robotic perception tasks.

**摘要:** TAIHRI是首个专为近距人机交互感知设计的视觉语言模型，通过将3D关键点量化为有限交互空间，利用2D关键点推理进行next token预测来实现精确的3D空间坐标定位，并能适配自然语言控制等下游任务。


## 📄 论文 4 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** ActFER: Agentic Facial Expression Recognition via Active Tool-Augmented Visual Reasoning
**作者:** Shifeng Liu, Zhengye Zhang, Sirui Zhao
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.08990

**相关性说明:** While the paper uses VLMs (a mentioned method), the application domain is fundamentally different. The paper focuses on 2D facial expression recognition (a classification/recognition task), not 3D motion generation or 2D-to-3D conversion. The research profile targets motion generation from video, quality control for 3D motions, and text-to-motion alignment - all generation tasks. Facial expression recognition is a perception task, not a generation task. Using VLMs for facial analysis is not the same as using VLMs for motion generation quality control.

**摘要:** ActFER是一种主动感知框架，将面部表情识别重新定义为主动视觉证据获取结合多模态推理。该框架通过动态调用工具进行人脸检测和对齐，选择性放大信息局部区域，并通过视觉思维链对面部动作单元和情绪进行推理。为此开发了UC-GRPO强化学习算法，使用基于动作单元的多层次可验证奖励进行监督，使用查询条件对比效用估计进行样本感知的动态信用分配，以及情绪感知的EMA校准来减少噪声效用估计。


## 📄 论文 5 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** PhysInOne: Visual Physics Learning and Reasoning in One Suite
**作者:** Siyuan Zhou, Hejun Wang, Hu Cheng
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.09415

**相关性说明:** The paper focuses on physics grounding for world models (physical phenomena like mechanics, optics, fluid dynamics) with applications in video generation and motion transfer, but NOT specifically on 2D video to 3D human/character motion conversion. The application domain is fundamentally different - it's about physics simulation and understanding rather than extracting motion from video for avatar/character generation. While it touches on video and motion, the core focus is on physical property understanding and prediction, not the specific pain points of 2D-to-3D motion extraction for character animation.

**摘要:** PhysInOne是一个大规模合成数据集，包含200万个视频和153,810个动态3D场景，涵盖71种基本物理现象，是同类中规模最大的数据集，为物理接地世界模型提供新的基准。
