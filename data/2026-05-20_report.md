## 📄 论文 1 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** GeoWorld-VLM: Geometry from World Models for Vision-Language Models
**作者:** Renjie Gu, Kaichen Zhou, Yan Luo
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.16713

**相关性说明:** While this paper deals with extracting 3D geometric structure from 2D visual inputs (related to pain point #4), it is fundamentally about improving VLM spatial reasoning for geometric relationships (left of, on, behind), NOT about Text-to-Motion / 3D Human Motion Generation. The application domain is different - it's about visual language models for spatial reasoning, not motion generation. However, this paper DOES use world models (related to the Seedance method mentioned in the profile) and addresses 3D structure extraction from 2D, which partially overlaps with bridging 2D visual features to 3D representations.

**摘要:** 本文提出GeoWorld-VLM框架，通过从冻结的相机条件视频世界模型中提取几何结构来增强视觉语言模型(VLM)对空间关系判断的能力。该方法仅微调图像编码器和多模态投影器，保持语言模型主干冻结，在保留原有语言能力的同时提升空间推理性能。在两个不同VLM架构上实现约4%的性能提升。


## 📄 论文 2 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** EVA01: Unified Native 3D Understanding and Generation via Mixture-of-Transformers
**作者:** Zongyuan Yang, Mingjing Yi, Wanli Ma
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.16745

**相关性说明:** The paper is about 3D MESH/OBJECT generation (text-to-3D), which is a DIFFERENT application domain from 3D HUMAN MOTION generation. While EVA01 uses advanced generation methods (MoT architecture), it targets static/dynamic 3D geometry (furniture, shapes) rather than human skeletal motion. The pain points in the research profile (video-to-motion extraction, VLM for motion quality control, kinematic representations) are NOT addressed. Both use 3D generation concepts, but the target output and domain are fundamentally different - one generates 3D objects, the other generates human movement/animations.

**摘要:** 本文提出EVA01框架，首次将3D网格作为原生模态整合到多模态大语言模型(MLLMs)中，通过混合Transformer架构解耦语义理解与几何推理，实现原生text-to-3D生成和长上下文多轮几何编辑。


## 📄 论文 3 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** HighSync: High-Quality Lip Synchronization via Latent Diffusion Models
**作者:** Saeed Firouzi Daghigh, Majid Iranpour Mobarekeh, Mostafa Alavi
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.16918

**相关性说明:** This paper addresses lip synchronization (facial animation) rather than 3D human motion generation. While it uses diffusion models (similar method), the application domain is fundamentally different - lip sync is about generating mouth movements to match audio for talking heads/avatars, not about extracting/general 3D human body motion from video or text. The research profile focuses on full 3D human motion generation with kinematic representations, while this paper focuses on audio-visual synthesis for lip movements. This is a different subdomain in computer graphics (facial animation vs. body motion generation).

**摘要:** HighSync是一个端到端的基于扩散模型的高保真唇同步框架，首次在512*512原生分辨率下实现Lip Sync，解决了图像质量与同步精度之间的权衡问题，并通过识别和消除数据泄露现象提升了时间建模能力。


## 📄 论文 4 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** RHINO: Reconstructing Human Interactions with Novel Objects from Monocular Videos
**作者:** Lixin Xue, Chengwei Zheng, Georgios Paschalidis
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.17014

**相关性说明:** This paper focuses on 3D reconstruction of human-object interactions from monocular videos (estimating existing motion), not Text-to-Motion generation. The application domain is different: reconstruction/estimation vs. generative motion synthesis. Methods are also different: it uses 3D-aware foundational models and neural SDFs, not diffusion models or VLMs for quality control. While it addresses extracting 3D information from 2D video (partial pain point), it doesn't address text-to-motion, multi-modal fusion challenges, or VLM-based quality control.

**摘要:** RHINO是一种从单目RGB视频中重建人物、新颖物体和静态场景的3D框架，通过三步法（3D感知基础模型 stabilization、相机运动补偿、神经场 refinement）实现高精度重建，并在新数据集上超越先进基线方法。


## 📄 论文 5 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** Markerless Motion Capture for Biomechanical Whole-Body Kinematic Estimation in Infants
**作者:** Divya Joshi, J. D. Peiffer, Colleen Peyton
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.17120

**相关性说明:** The paper addresses markerless motion capture (pose estimation) for infants, which is fundamentally different from Text-to-Motion/3D Motion Generation. The research profile focuses on GENERATING new motions via diffusion models, VLMs, and multi-modal learning, while this paper deals with motion CAPTURE/estimation from video using pose estimation frameworks (MeTRAbs-ACAE, SAM 3D Body, Sapiens) and inverse kinematics. This is a estimation/capture task in the medical/biomechanical domain, not a generation task in computer graphics. The only partial connection is the general theme of extracting 3D motion from 2D video, but the methodology (pose estimation vs diffusion-based generation) and application domain (clinical biomechanics vs generative graphics) are different.

**摘要:** 研究评估了三种计算机视觉姿态估计方法在婴儿运动分析中的表现，发现Sapiens重投影误差最低，SAM 3D Body最适合运动学重建，为婴儿运动发育的自动评估提供了基础
