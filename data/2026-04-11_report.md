## 📄 论文 2 - 相关性: 50%
**标题:** PolySLGen: Online Multimodal Speaking-Listening Reaction Generation in Polyadic Interaction
**作者:** Zhi-Yi Lin, Thomas Markhorst, Jouh Yeong Chew
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.08125

**相关性说明:** This paper is about generating multimodal reactions (speech, body movements, speaking states) for multiple people in social interactions. While it involves human motion generation (relevant to Computer Graphics), the core focus is on conversational/avatar reactions in polyadic interactions rather than 3D motion generation from video features. The methods used (pose fusion module, social cue encoder) don't involve diffusion models, VLMs, or the specific video-to-motion pipeline mentioned in the research profile. It addresses some aspects of temporal consistency and motion generation, but misses the key pain points about domain gap between 2D video features and 3D motion generation, and visual guidance feature extraction.

**摘要:** PolySLGen是一个用于多人群多模态说话和倾听反应生成的在线框架，通过姿态融合模块和社会线索编码器聚合群体动作和社会信号，生成包含语音、身体动作和说话状态分数的多模态反应，在多个指标上优于现有方法。


## 📄 论文 3 - 相关性: 45%
**标题:** DP-DeGauss: Dynamic Probabilistic Gaussian Decomposition for Egocentric 4D Scene Reconstruction
**作者:** Tingxi Chen, Zhengxue Cheng, Houqiang Zhong
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.07986

**相关性说明:** While both papers are in Computer Graphics, the application domains are different. This paper focuses on 4D scene reconstruction using 3D Gaussian Splatting for egocentric views, while the research profile is about video-to-motion generation (extracting motion from video for 3D motion models). The methods are also different - this paper uses Gaussian decomposition rather than diffusion models or transformers for motion generation. It's moderately relevant as it addresses 3D reconstruction but doesn't directly address the pain points of video-to-motion domain gap, visual feature extraction for motion models, or temporal consistency in motion generation.

**摘要:** DP-DeGauss是一种用于自我中心4D重建的动态概率高斯分解框架，通过将统一的3D高斯集合初始化并动态路由到专门化的 deformation branches 中，实现背景、手和物体的分离。
