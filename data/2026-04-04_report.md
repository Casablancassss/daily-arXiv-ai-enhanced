# 📊 每日 arXiv 论文推荐

**研究画像:**
- 领域: Computer Graphics - 3D Human Motion Generation
- 方法: Visual Language Models (VLM) for quality supervision, Video generation models (Wanxiang, Seedance), 3D motion generation models, Feature extraction pipelines
- 痛点: Domain gap between 2D video and 3D motion representations, Ensuring temporal consistency in visual-to-motion translation, Quality control of generated video content, Extracting reliable visual guidance features from 2D videos

**最相关的 5 篇论文:**


## 📄 论文 1 - 相关性: 45%
**标题:** Control-DINO: Feature Space Conditioning for Controllable Image-to-Video Diffusion
**作者:** Edoardo A. Dominici, Thomas Deixelberger, Konstantinos Vardis
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.01761

**相关性说明:** This paper focuses on Image-to-Video diffusion for video generation, not 3D Human Motion Generation. While both use diffusion models, the application domains are completely different: the paper generates video frames from images, whereas the research profile is about generating 3D skeletal motion trajectories from visual inputs. The paper does address feature extraction (DINO features) and quality control, which tangentially relates to some pain points, but it doesn't address the core challenge of 2D video to 3D motion translation or temporal consistency for human motion generation. The methods (video diffusion) are similar but applied to a different output domain.

**摘要:** 本文提出利用自监督学习特征（如DINO）作为预训练视频扩散模型的通用 conditioning 信号，实现视频域转换和3D视频生成。通过引入轻量级架构和解耦策略，解耦外观与场景其他特征，实现robust的外观控制（如风格化和重光照）。


## 📄 论文 2 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** VOID: Video Object and Interaction Deletion
**作者:** Saman Motamed, William Harvey, Benjamin Klein
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.02296

**相关性说明:** Despite some method overlap (VLM guidance, video diffusion, physical consistency), this paper addresses VIDEO OBJECT REMOVAL - a video editing task, NOT 3D Human Motion Generation. The application domain is fundamentally different: the paper removes unwanted objects from existing videos, whereas the research profile needs to GENERATE 3D motion from 2D video inputs. While both involve video diffusion and VLMs, the end goal differs significantly - video editing vs. motion generation. The mentioned HUMOTO dataset usage is for object interaction data, not motion generation.

**摘要:** VOID是一个视频目标移除框架，通过生成反事实配对数据集、视觉语言模型引导和视频扩散模型，实现物理上合理的目标移除，特别擅长处理物体间的碰撞等复杂物理交互场景。


## 📄 论文 3 - 相关性: 30%
**标题:** Director: Instance-aware Gaussian Splatting for Dynamic Scene Modeling and Understanding
**作者:** Yuheng Jiang, Yiwen Cai, Zihao Wang
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.01678

**相关性说明:** This paper focuses on Gaussian Splatting for 4D scene reconstruction and rendering (computer graphics), not 3D human motion generation. While it addresses temporal consistency and uses VLMs, the application domain is fundamentally different - it's about rendering quality and 4D reconstruction, not about generating human motion from 2D videos. The pain points in the profile relate to video-to-motion translation, while this paper deals with instance-aware scene modeling for rendering purposes.

**摘要:** 本文提出Director，一个统一的时空高斯表示框架，联合建模人体动作、高保真渲染和实例级语义。该方法利用多模态大语言模型生成的实例掩码和句子嵌入来监督高斯语义特征，实现语言对齐的4D表示并保持身份一致性。


## 📄 论文 4 - 相关性: 25%
**标题:** Human Pose Estimation in Trampoline Gymnastics: Improving Performance Using a New Synthetic Dataset
**作者:** Léa Drolet-Roy, Victor Nogues, Sylvain Gaudet
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.01322

**相关性说明:** This paper focuses on Human Pose Estimation (2D/3D), which is the inverse problem of motion generation. While it involves human pose, the application domain is fundamentally different - pose estimation goes from image to pose, while the research profile is about generating 3D motion from visual inputs (video-to-motion translation). The paper doesn't address any of the specified pain points (temporal consistency, quality control, visual guidance features for generation) and uses estimation methods (ViTPose) rather than generation methods (diffusion models for motion).

**摘要:** 本文提出使用合成 trampoline poses (STP) 数据集微调 ViTPose 模型来解决体操动作姿态估计难题。


## 📄 论文 5 - 相关性: 25%
**标题:** GRAZE: Grounded Refinement and Motion-Aware Zero-Shot Event Localization
**作者:** Syed Ahsan Masud Zaidi, Lior Shamir, William Hsu
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.01383

**相关性说明:** This paper is about American football training video analysis for localizing contact points (FPOC), NOT about 3D human motion generation. While it shares some general topics like motion understanding and temporal reasoning, the application domain is completely different - it's sports video analytics for biomechanical analysis, not computer graphics motion generation. The methods (Grounding DINO, SAM2) are for object detection and segmentation in videos, not for generating 3D human motion from videos. The pain points being addressed (domain gap between 2D video and 3D motion, visual-to-motion translation) are unrelated to this paper's goal of locating contact events in football videos.

**摘要:** 本文研究美式足球训练视频中首次接触点(FPOC)的定位问题。提出了GRAZE管道，无需标注数据即可实现训练自由的接触定位。该方法结合Grounding DINO发现候选交互、时序推理细化、SAM2像素级验证，在738个训练视频上取得97.4%有效输出率，±10帧准确率达77.5%，±20帧准确率达82.7%。
