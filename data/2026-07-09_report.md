## 📄 论文 1 - 相关性: 25%
**标题:** ELSA3D: Elastic Semantic Anchoring for Unified 3D Understanding and Generation
**作者:** Tianjiao Yu, Xinzhuo Li, Yifan Shen
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.06565

**相关性说明:** The paper addresses 3D object generation (static) while the research profile is about 3D MOTION generation (temporal/dynamic). Even though both are in Computer Graphics, they are different application domains. The paper focuses on text-to-3D and image-to-3D generation of shapes/objects, not 3D motion synthesis from video priors. The methods (elastic semantic anchoring, octree tokenizer) target static 3D understanding/generation, not the video-to-motion pipeline or motion synthesis from VLMs described in the profile.

**摘要:** ELSA3D是一种统一的3D基础模型，通过弹性语义锚（Elastic Semantic Anchoring）机制解决文本-3D交互中隐式建模和粗细粒度信息混淆的问题，实现语言与几何推理在匹配抽象尺度上的联合结构化。


## 📄 论文 2 - 相关性: 15%
**标题:** CanvasAgent: Enabling Complex Image Creation and Editing via Visual Tool Orchestration
**作者:** Hairui Zhu, Yiying Yang, Tengjin Weng
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.05465

**相关性说明:** This paper is about 2D image creation and editing using multi-modal agents, which is a completely different application domain from the research profile's focus on 3D human motion generation from video priors. While both involve visual models, the end goal is entirely different - the paper focuses on orchestrating visual tools for image generation/editing, not on extracting 3D guidance features from video for motion synthesis or bridging 2D-to-3D domain gaps. This represents a fundamental mismatch in application domain.

**摘要:** CanvasCraft是一个大规模多模态工具使用数据集，包含14万条带完整注释的可执行轨迹和1万条强化学习任务规范。CanvasAgent是一个工具增强的多模态代理，通过多轮交互协调异构视觉工具。代理首先通过SFT学习可执行推理-动作轨迹，然后使用结合结果级和过程级信号的混合奖励进行GRPO优化。

## 📄 论文 5 - 相关性: 100%
**标题:** RMISC: A Large-scale Real-world Multivariate Corpus for Time Series Foundation Models
**作者:** Qian Sun, Yong-Ming Tian, Jia-Wei Huang
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.06504

**相关性说明:** This paper is about Time Series Foundation Models (TSFMs) for forecasting and analyzing multivariate time series data. The application domain is completely different from the research profile which focuses on Computer Graphics, specifically 3D motion generation from video/image priors. The paper addresses time series analysis, not video-to-motion generation, visual quality supervision, or text-driven motion synthesis. There is no connection to Vision Language Models for quality supervision, video generation models (Wanxiang/Seedance), or downstream 3D motion generation pipelines. This is a case of completely different application domains - time series forecasting vs. computer graphics motion synthesis.

**摘要:** 本文研究了时间序列基础模型（TSFMs）在真实世界多变量数据与合成数据上的预训练效果差异，并构建了包含约200个数据集和1420亿时间点的大规模RMISC语料库。实验表明，真实世界多变量数据能显著提升模型的零样本泛化能力。
