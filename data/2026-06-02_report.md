## 📄 论文 1 - 相关性: 68%
**标题:** Omni-Supervised Motion Editing: Balancing Change and Invariance through Positive-Negative Learning
**作者:** Zhenwu Shi, Jingyu Gong, Peiwei Wang
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30969

**相关性说明:** This paper directly addresses Text-to-Motion in Computer Graphics (text-driven motion editing), which matches the core application domain. It tackles semantic alignment between text and motion, which aligns with one of the pain points. However, it misses several key aspects: no VLM-based supervision (a major pain point), no cross-domain 2D video to 3D geometry transfer, doesn't address limited training data, and doesn't use the specific methods mentioned (video generation models, VLMs for assessment, diffusion models for motion synthesis). The approach uses positive-negative learning rather than diffusion-based motion synthesis. It's highly relevant to motion editing but less so to the broader research profile focusing on video-to-3D motion and VLM supervision.

**摘要:** 本文提出OmniME框架，解决文本驱动动作编辑中平衡"变化"与"不变性"的挑战，通过三种互补监督组件实现高质量动作编辑。


## 📄 论文 2 - 相关性: 55%
**标题:** MultiAct: Text-to-Motion Generation from Composite Text via Tailored Attention Guidance
**作者:** Nathan Sala, Ofir Abramovich, Ariel Shamir
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30925

**相关性说明:** While this paper IS in the Text-to-Motion domain (matching the general field), it addresses a different problem - handling composite text prompts with multiple simultaneous actions through attention guidance. It does not address the key pain points mentioned in the profile: (1) No cross-domain knowledge transfer from 2D video to 3D motion geometry, (2) No VLM-based quality assessment, (3) No extraction of 3D geometric cues from 2D video priors. The methods are also different - this uses attention guidance on pre-trained models rather than Video Generation Models or VLMs for 3D motion extraction. It's moderately relevant as it's in the same general domain but doesn't target the specific challenges outlined in the research profile.

**摘要:** MultiAct是一个无需重新训练的组合式文本到动作合成推理框架，通过自适应增强被忽略的提示词成分的交叉注意力分数，解决现有模型处理多重同时动作描述时的语义崩溃问题。


## 📄 论文 3 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** SLAP: The Semantic Least Action Principle for Variational Video-Language Modeling
**作者:** Xiang Fang, Wanlong Fang
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30750

**相关性说明:** This paper addresses video frame interpolation using a variational/physics-inspired approach on Riemannian manifolds. While it deals with temporal consistency and semantic consistency (touching some pain points), the APPLICATION DOMAIN is fundamentally different: it's about video interpolation (2D visual content), not Text-to-Motion (3D human motion generation). The paper doesn't generate 3D motion geometry from text, nor does it address extracting 3D geometric cues from 2D video. It's in the video generation domain rather than the motion generation domain. Even though both involve temporal modeling, the output and goals are categorically different - this is more related to video generation (like Wanxiang/Seedance) than to text-to-motion synthesis.

**摘要:** 本文提出语义最小作用原理(SLAP)，将视频插帧从概率生成范式转向变分力学方法，将潜在视频轨迹建模为黎曼流形上的路径，通过离散欧拉-拉格朗日方程求解边值问题，实现物体持久性和语义一致性。


## 📄 论文 4 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** CameraNoise: Enabling Faithful Camera Control in Video Diffusion through Geometry-Flow-Guided Noise Warping
**作者:** Haoyu Zhao, Jiaxi Gu, Haoran Chen
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30774

**相关性说明:** While both papers involve diffusion models and geometry-related methods, they target fundamentally different application domains. The research profile focuses on TEXT-TO-MOTION (generating 3D human motions from text), whereas CameraNoise addresses VIDEO generation with camera control. The paper does touch on geometry extraction and temporal consistency - which align partially with some pain points (extracting geometric cues from 2D, temporal coherence). However, it lacks key elements: no text-to-motion generation, no semantic alignment between text and visual motion features, and no VLM-based quality assessment. The main contribution is camera pose control in video diffusion, which is orthogonal to motion synthesis. Using similar diffusion methods in different domains (video generation vs motion generation) makes this only modestly relevant (similar methodology but wrong application).

**摘要:** 提出CameraNoise方法，通过将相机姿态编码到噪声空间中实现视频扩散的精确相机位姿控制，保持几何一致性。


## 📄 论文 5 - 相关性: 35%
**标题:** Robust Dreamer: Deviation-Aware Latent Gaussian Memory for Action-Controlled AR Video Generation
**作者:** Hanlin Chen, Jiaxin Wei, Xibin Song
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30855

**相关性说明:** This paper is about 3D-aware Image-to-Video generation (specifically AR video synthesis from images), NOT Text-to-Motion generation. While it uses diffusion models and addresses temporal consistency, the application domain is completely different - it generates video sequences from images for view synthesis, not human motion from text descriptions. The paper focuses on long-term video generation with 3D consistency, but this is for scene-level video synthesis (e.g., ScanNet, DL3DV datasets) rather than character motion generation needed in the research profile. Some tangential relevance exists around cross-domain knowledge transfer (2D→3D) and temporal consistency, but these are too distant from the core Text-to-Motion goal.

**摘要:** Robust Dreamer是一个基于记忆增强的3D aware图像到视频生成框架，通过潜空间高斯记忆和动态偏差存档技术解决长时序自回归生成中的视觉退化和3D一致性问题。
