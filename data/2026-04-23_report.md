## 📄 论文 1 - 相关性: 82%
**标题:** UniCon3R: Contact-aware 3D Human-Scene Reconstruction from Monocular Video
**作者:** Tanuj Sur, Shashank Tripathi, Nikos Athanasiou
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.19923

**相关性说明:** This paper directly addresses the core research field of Computer Graphics / Human Motion Generation. It tackles critical pain points: extracting 3D motion from 2D video (monocular video), bridging the domain gap between 2D video and 3D motion, and ensuring quality/accuracy through physical plausibility. While it uses a reconstruction approach (not diffusion-based generation), it is the EXACT application domain needed. The method addresses physically plausible 3D human motion estimation from video, which is a key goal in this field.

**摘要:** UniCon3R是一个统一的在线人类场景4D重建框架,通过显式建模人体与场景的物理交互来解决现有方法中身体悬浮或穿透场景等物理不合理的问题。该方法从人体姿态和场景几何中推断3D接触,并将接触作为纠正线索来生成最终姿态。
